Dalaman Sulfur Springs — Natural Health Resort with the Scent of Health
A few kilometers from the center of Dalaman, surrounded by greenery, lies one of its hidden gems — thermal sulfur springs. This is not a modern spa complex, but rather a natural, "folk" health resort where people have been coming for centuries to improve their health with the help of healing water and mud gifted by nature itself.

Main feature: High sulfur content and healing properties
Healing water: The main asset of this place is water with a very high content of sulfur and other minerals. It has a characteristic yellowish-green tint and a specific smell of hydrogen sulfide ("rotten eggs"), which is said to be an indicator of its healing power. Swimming in this water is believed to help in the treatment of skin diseases (eczema, psoriasis), rheumatism, and joint pain.

Healing mud: The bottom of the pools is covered with a layer of healing mud, which is also used for application on the body.

What to expect from a visit
There are several pools on the territory of the complex:

Main pool: A large outdoor pool where thermal water flows at a temperature of about 28-30°C.

Indoor facilities: There are also separate small indoor pools for men and women.

Drinking water source: There is a separate tap with running sulfur water that can be drunk (considered beneficial for the stomach, but the taste is very specific).

Atmosphere and infrastructure
Atmosphere: Very simple, authentic, and far from tourist gloss. People come here primarily for health, not for luxury. This is a place where you can see real, non-tourist life and chat with locals.

Infrastructure: Very basic. There are simple changing rooms, showers, and toilets. A small tea garden (çay bahçesi) works nearby, where you can drink tea or have a snack.

Pros and cons
Pros:

Real, natural healing water with a high sulfur content.

Authentic, non-tourist experience.

Very low cost of visit.

Potential health benefits.

Cons:

Very strong and specific smell of hydrogen sulfide.

Infrastructure is very simple, without any comfort.

Muddy color of the water may confuse some visitors.

Who are the Sulfur Springs suitable for?
This place is for those looking for health, not entertainment, and for curious travelers who want to see the "real" Turkey. If you suffer from skin or joint diseases or simply want to experience the power of natural mineral springs in their original form, you will like it here. If you are looking for a beautiful spa complex with perfect service, then this place will most likely not suit you.
