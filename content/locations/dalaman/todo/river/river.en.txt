# Dalaman River (Dalaman Çayı) — Adrenaline Rafting and Taurus Canyons

The Dalaman River is a fast-flowing mountain river running through deep canyons, celebrated as one of the premier rafting destinations in southwestern Turkey.

### Rafting Adventures
Rafting is the main highlight on the Dalaman River. Offering rapid classes ranging from R2 to R4, the river accommodates both seasoned rafters and absolute beginners. Commercial rafting tours run from May to October as all-inclusive experiences. A standard tour covers hotel transfer, breakfast, a thorough safety briefing, professional gear (helmets, life jackets, and wetsuits when needed), a 2 to 3-hour guided descent, and a hearty lunch at the base.

### Travel and Base Facilities
Rafting bases are tucked deep in the mountains, accessed by narrow, unpaved dirt roads. Driving there independently in a rental sedan is highly discouraged; it is best to use the organized transfer included with your booking. The base camps offer changing rooms, hot showers, toilets, and dining areas.

### Practical Information
* **Location:** Rafting bases are located in the mountains 30-50 km from [Dalaman](/en/dalaman) (transfers cover hotels in Fethiye, Marmaris, [Gocek](/en/gocek), and Dalaman).
* **Road:** Winding mountain roads (asphalt on main highways, rough dirt/gravel tracks close to the bases).
* **Infrastructure:** Changing rooms, showers, toilets, restaurants, and gear rental at the base. The river itself flows through wild canyons.
* **Entrance:** Free access to the river; commercial rafting packages in 2026 cost around 2,900 TL (~90–100 USD), including transfers and meals.
* **Tip:** Wear tightly fitting water shoes (flip-flops will get swept away by rapids) and bring a change of dry clothes. Apply water-resistant sunscreen. Do not bring glasses or phones unless they are securely strapped.
