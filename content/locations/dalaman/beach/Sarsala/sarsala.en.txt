Sarsala Cove — Dalaman's Hidden Paradise, Loved by Locals and Yachts
Sarsala Cove is a treasure hidden at the end of a winding mountain road. This is a place where you can experience the true, non-touristic atmosphere of the Aegean coast. The cove is famous for its pristine nature, crystal-clear water, and its status as a favorite stop for "blue cruises" and private yachts.

Main feature: Untouched nature and calm sea
Sarsala is located in a deep, well-protected bay surrounded by steep hills covered with thick pine forest. Thanks to this location, the sea here is almost always calm, like in a pool, and there are no large waves. The water is incredibly transparent and has a beautiful turquoise hue. This is an ideal place for those who want to enjoy swimming in calm and clean water surrounded by untouched nature.

Beach and atmosphere
Coastline: The beach is small, mainly pebbly, with small areas of sand.

Atmosphere: Dual. On weekdays, especially in the morning, silence and peace reign here. However, on weekends the cove is transformed, filling up with local families who come here for picnics, and tourists from numerous excursion yachts that make a stop here.

Infrastructure: The only cafe on the beach is operated by the municipality, where you can buy drinks and simple snacks at very reasonable prices. There are also basic amenities: toilets, showers, and changing cabins. You can rent sunbeds and umbrellas.

How to get there
By land: The road from Dalaman (about 17 km) is an adventure in itself. It is very scenic, passes through pine forests and offers stunning views of Lake Kocagöl, but it is quite narrow and winding, requiring careful driving.

By sea: Sarsala is a must-visit point on the itinerary of most daily boat trips from Göcek, known as the "12 Islands".

Pros and cons
Pros:

Stunning natural beauty and very clean, calm water.

A feeling of a "secret", non-touristic place.

An ideal place for swimming and snorkeling.

Affordable prices in the municipal cafe.

Cons:

Difficult and winding road.

It can be very crowded on weekends and during the stops of excursion boats.

Pebble beach, which is not to everyone's liking.

Who is Sarsala suitable for?
Sarsala is an ideal choice for those traveling by car who want to discover not the most obvious, but very beautiful places. It will appeal to lovers of nature, clean sea, and authentic atmosphere. It is a wonderful place for a day trip to escape the hustle and bustle and enjoy one of the most beautiful "secret" beaches in the vicinity of Dalaman.
