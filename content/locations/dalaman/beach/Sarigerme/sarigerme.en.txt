# Sarigerme Beach (Sarıgerme Plajı) — Golden Sands and Perfect Family Getaway

Sarigerme Beach is one of the region's finest sandy beaches with a shallow entry and a Blue Flag, making it an excellent choice for family holidays.

### Beach Highlights
Sarigerme is famous for its multi-kilometer stretch of fine golden sand. The beach is exceptionally wide, the sea is clean and open, which often brings a refreshing breeze and light waves. The central and most popular part is managed by the SARÇED association. Renowned for its ecological cleanliness and safety, the beach is regularly awarded the prestigious Blue Flag. While major resorts line the coast, the SARÇED municipal area is open to all visitors.

### Facilities
The recreation area is fully equipped with a cafeteria serving snacks and drinks, toilets, showers, changing cabins, lifeguards, playgrounds, and a grassy picnic zone. The road leading to the beach from [Dalaman](/en/dalaman) is completely paved.

### Practical Information
* **Location:** 12 km from Ortaca center, 15 km from [Dalaman](/en/dalaman) airport.
* **Road:** Asphalt.
* **Infrastructure:** Cafe, toilets, showers, changing cabins, sunbeds and umbrellas, playground, picnic area, lifeguards.
* **Entrance:** Paid. A small entry fee is charged for pedestrians and vehicles (approx. 300 TL for car parking).
* **Tip:** An ideal spot for families with young children due to the soft sand and shallow shoreline. Bring plenty of sun protection as there is no natural shade on the sand.
