Sarigerme — Golden Sands and Turquoise Sea
Sarigerme is a paradise for lovers of sandy beaches. Unlike many pebbly coves on the Aegean coast, Sarigerme is famous for its multi-kilometer stretch of the finest, golden sand that gently slopes into a crystal-clear, turquoise sea. This is a place where you can walk barefoot for hours, enjoying the vast space and beauty.

Main feature: Perfect sand and organized public beach
7 kilometers of sand: The main treasure of Sarigerme is its beach. It is very long and wide (up to 100 meters in some places), so it is never crowded. The sand is fine and soft, and the entry into the sea is very shallow and safe, making it ideal for families with children.

SARÇED: The central and most popular part of the beach is managed by the local association for environmental protection and tourism (SARÇED). Thanks to their work, the beach is impeccably clean, well-maintained, and has all the necessary infrastructure. For this cleanliness and organization, the beach regularly receives the "Blue Flag" award.

Beach and atmosphere
Coastline: Open sea, so there is often a light breeze and small waves here, which is refreshing in the heat and pleases lovers of water sports such as windsurfing and kitesurfing.

Atmosphere: Sarigerme is more of a resort area than a town. The atmosphere here is very quiet, relaxed, and family-oriented. Behind the beach is a small village with one main street lined with shops, restaurants, and bars. The majority of holidaymakers are guests of large, mostly all-inclusive hotels located along the coast.

Infrastructure
The SARÇED public beach offers:

Paid entry: A small fee is charged for entry to the territory, which goes towards maintaining cleanliness and infrastructure.

Full range of services: Rental of sunbeds and umbrellas, showers, toilets, changing cabins.

Cafe: A large cafe with reasonable prices.

Additional entertainment: A small zoo and picnic areas.

Pros and cons
Pros:

Gorgeous, one of the best sandy beaches in Turkey.

Very clean, well-maintained, and safe, thanks to the work of SARÇED.

Ideally suited for families with children due to the fine sand and shallow entry.

Proximity to Dalaman airport (only 15-20 minutes away).

Cons:

It can be windy with waves, which not everyone likes.

Relative isolation: The village of Sarigerme is small, and there is little entertainment outside of it.

Paid entry to the central, equipped part of the beach.

Who is Sarigerme suitable for?
Sarigerme is an ideal place for a classic beach holiday. It is the number one choice for families with children and for anyone who values a quality, clean, and spacious sandy beach above all else. If you are looking for a peaceful holiday in a large comfortable hotel on the beachfront, or simply want to spend the day on one of Turkey's best sandy beaches, Sarigerme will not disappoint you.
