Kayacik Beach — Sunsets and Planes on the Shores of Dalaman
Kayacik Beach is the main public beach in Dalaman, featuring a truly unique characteristic that sets it apart from all others. This is a place where you can combine a relaxed beach holiday with a thrilling spectacle — planespotting, which is watching planes take off and land.

Main feature: Proximity to the airport
The beach is located in close proximity to the runway of Dalaman Airport. Every few minutes, huge airliners roar directly over the heads of beachgoers. For some, this might seem like a drawback, but for most, it is a unique and exciting attraction that adds a special touch to the holiday. It is, perhaps, the best place in Turkey for aviation enthusiasts and those looking to take spectacular photos.

Beach and atmosphere
Coastline: It is a long and wide beach consisting of a mix of sand and pebbles. The sea is open here, so there are often waves, which attracts surf enthusiasts in windy weather.

Atmosphere: Very casual and popular with locals. It is not a secluded cove, but rather a large public recreation area. The beach especially comes to life in the evening, as it is famous for its incredibly beautiful sunsets.

Infrastructure: The beach is well-equipped by the Dalaman municipality. There is a large cafe with very affordable prices, showers, toilets, changing cabins, and a large parking lot. Also, the famous swing and other photogenic installations are set up on the beach, where there is always a queue.

What to do
Spotting: Watch planes.

Sunset watching: Kayacik is one of the best places in the region to watch the sun go down.

Photoshoots: Use the swing and other installations to create beautiful pictures against the backdrop of the sea and sunset.

Beach recreation: Swim and sunbathe.

Pros and cons
Pros:

Unique opportunity to watch airplanes.

Stunningly beautiful sunsets.

Excellent municipal infrastructure and very affordable prices.

Wide and spacious beach.

Cons:

Constant noise from airplanes (for those seeking peace).

Often waves, which is not always suitable for families with small children.

The water quality might be inferior to that of secluded coves.

Who is Kayacik Beach suitable for?
Kayacik is an ideal place for those seeking unusual experiences. It will appeal to aviation enthusiasts, photographers, and anyone who wants to spend the evening admiring a gorgeous sunset in a lively and casual atmosphere. It is a wonderful option to pass the time before a flight from Dalaman Airport, or simply for a day trip to see something new and capture some truly unique frames.
