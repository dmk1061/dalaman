# Kayacik Beach (Kayacık Plajı) — Plane Spotting and Wild Coastal Nature

Kayacik Beach is a spacious municipal beach in Dalaman next to the airport runway, offering fantastic opportunities for plane spotting and scenic sunsets.

### Beach and Sea
The beach is wide and long, consisting of a mix of sand and pebbles. The sea is open and clean, though the lack of a sheltered bay means waves and wind are common. The spot is highly popular with locals, especially in the evening, as it offers a panoramic sunset view. Swings and art installations are set up on the shore for photos.

### Infrastructure
The beach is fully managed by the [Dalaman](/en/dalaman) Municipality (Dalaman Belediyesi). There is a budget-friendly municipal cafe, along with showers, toilets, and changing cabins. The road from [Dalaman](/en/dalaman) city center is fully paved and suitable for all types of vehicles.

### Practical Information
* **Location:** 10 km from [Dalaman](/en/dalaman) city center (approx. 10 minutes by car).
* **Road:** Asphalt.
* **Infrastructure:** Municipal cafe, toilets, showers, changing cabins, sunbed and umbrella rentals, playground.
* **Entrance:** Free, parking is free.
* **Tip:** Visit in the late afternoon to catch the sunset and photograph planes in the evening light. Due to waves and wind, it is not the most suitable choice for families with toddlers.
