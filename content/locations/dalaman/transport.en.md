---
title: "Transport in Dalaman"
slug: dalaman-transport
---

Dalaman is the region's largest transport hub, providing access to the popular resorts of Turkey's southwestern coast. The transport system here is well developed and offers options for any budget.

### **How to get to Dalaman**

#### **By plane**
**Dalaman International Airport (DLM)** is the main way to get to the region. It is located just 6 km from the center of Dalaman city and serves both domestic and numerous international flights, especially during the tourist season.

#### **From the airport to the resorts**
*   **Havaş and MUTTAŞ shuttle buses:** This is the most popular and budget-friendly way to get to major cities.
    *   **Havaş:** Buses depart approximately 25-30 minutes after the arrival of each domestic flight and head to the bus stations (Otogar) in Marmaris and Fethiye.
    *   **MUTTAŞ:** Municipal buses also run routes to Marmaris, Fethiye, and (seasonally) Datça.
*   **Taxi:** The taxi stand is located right at the exit from the terminals. This is the fastest, but also the most expensive way to get to your hotel.
*   **Transfer:** Many private companies offer private or group transfer services, which can be booked in advance online.
*   **Car rental:** Offices of both international and local rental companies are represented at the airport. Renting a car is an excellent choice if you plan to travel actively around the region.

### **Moving around the region**

#### **Intercity buses**
From **Dalaman Bus Station (Dalaman Otogar)**, located in the city center, you can reach almost any point in Turkey by comfortable buses. To get to the bus station from the airport, you need to take a taxi or a local minibus.

#### **Dolmuşes (Minibuses)**
Dolmuşes are the backbone of local public transport. These minibuses run on clearly defined routes with high frequency. You can use them to cheaply travel from Dalaman to neighboring cities and towns, such as Ortaca, Dalyan, Göcek, and Fethiye. Stopping is possible almost anywhere on request.

#### **Water transport**
From coastal towns such as Göcek and Fethiye, boat tours and water taxis regularly depart, which can take you to secluded coves and islands.

**Important:** There is no railway connection in the Dalaman region. The nearest railway station is in the city of Denizli.
