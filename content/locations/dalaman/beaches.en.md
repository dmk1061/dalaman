The coastline in the Dalaman region is famous for its clean beaches, secluded coves, and turquoise sea. Although the city of Dalaman itself is located slightly inland from the coast, the region's best beaches can be reached in a matter of minutes.

### [🏖️ <u>**Sarigerme Beach (Sarıgerme Plajı)**</u>](/dalaman/beach/Sarigerme)

Sarigerme is perhaps the most famous beach in the vicinity of Dalaman, located just 12 km from the city center.

*   **Description:** It is a wide, 7-kilometer beach with fine golden sand and a shallow entry into the sea, making it ideal for families with children. The beach is surrounded by a pine forest, creating a pleasant shade and aroma.
*   **Infrastructure:** The beach is excellently equipped. There are sunbeds, umbrellas, showers, changing cabins, and toilets. There are cafes on the territory where you can have a bite to eat. There is also a water sports center.
*   **Features:** For its cleanliness and environmental friendliness, Sarigerme Beach regularly receives the "Blue Flag" award.

### [🏊‍♂️ <u>**Sarsala Beach (Sarsala Plajı)**</u>](/dalaman/beach/Sarsala)

Sarsala is a charming and more secluded cove hidden among hills covered with pine trees.

*   **Description:** A small but very picturesque pebble-sand beach with crystal clear and calm water. This is a great place for those looking for peace and connection with nature.
*   **How to get there:** The road to the beach passes through scenic mountain switchbacks. It is located about 16 km from Dalaman.
*   **Infrastructure:** There is a small restaurant on the beach offering fresh seafood and local dishes. You can rent sunbeds and umbrellas.

### [⛵ <u>**Kille Cove (Kille Koyu)**</u>](/dalaman/beach/Kayacik)

Another beautiful cove popular among lovers of camping and wild recreation.

*   **Description:** A spacious cove with a sand-and-pebble beach and incredibly clear water. Surrounded by a dense forest, which creates a feeling of privacy.
*   **Features:** It is a popular spot for yacht anchorages. Excursion boats often stop here. A great place for snorkeling.

### **Other beaches and coves nearby**

Thanks to its convenient location, it is easy to get to other famous beaches in the region from Dalaman:

*   **Iztuzu Beach:** Located in neighboring Dalyan, this unique 5-kilometer sandy beach is a nesting site for Caretta Caretta sea turtles.
*   **Coves of Göcek:** From the port of Göcek, daily boat tours around the "12 Islands" depart, during which you can swim in the most beautiful and secluded coves, such as **Hamam (Cleopatra's Bath)**, **Göbün**, and **Boynuzbükü**.
