Iztuzu Beach — Turtle Beach, Where a Fresh River Meets the Salty Sea
Iztuzu Beach is not just a strip of sand, but a true miracle of nature. It is a 4.5-kilometer sand spit that serves as a natural barrier between the Mediterranean Sea on one side and the freshwater delta of the Dalyan River on the other. This creates a unique opportunity to swim in salty sea water, and then, after walking for just a couple of minutes, take a dip in the cool fresh river.

Main feature: Two waters and Caretta Caretta turtles
Two in one: The ability to swim in both the sea and the river is a unique experience that no other beach in the region offers.

Turtle sanctuary: The main value and fame of Iztuzu is that the beach is one of the world's largest nesting sites for the rare Loggerhead sea turtles (Caretta Caretta). From May to September, females come ashore to lay their eggs in the sand. Because of this, the beach has the status of a sanctuary and strict rules apply to it.

A beach with strict rules
Since preserving the turtle population is a priority, special rules apply on the beach:

The beach is closed to visitors from 20:00 in the evening until 08:00 in the morning so as not to disturb the turtles.

Egg nesting sites are fenced off with special pyramids. They cannot be approached, and it is forbidden to set up umbrellas and dig sand nearby.

Pets are not allowed on the beach.

Boats are prohibited from coming close to the shore.

Two ends of the beach
There are two ways to get to Iztuzu, and each will lead you to a different part of it:

"Boat end": Cooperative shuttle boats from the center of Dalyan arrive here. The famous Sea Turtle Rescue, Research and Rehabilitation Center (DEKAMER) is located here, where you can see injured turtles undergoing treatment and learn more about these amazing animals.

"Car end": You can drive here by car or take a dolmuş minibus from Dalyan. There is a more developed infrastructure here with a cafe and a large number of sunbeds.

Pros and cons
Pros:

Unique natural landscape (sea and river).

Opportunity to see rare sea turtles in their natural habitat (or in the rehabilitation center).

Perfect fine golden sand and a shallow entry into the sea.

A sense of involvement in the important cause of nature conservation.

Cons:

Strict rules limit some beach activities.

You cannot stay on the beach at sunset or at night.

It takes time to get from Dalyan (by boat or bus).

Who is Iztuzu suitable for?
Iztuzu is a must-visit place for everyone vacationing in the Dalyan, Fethiye or even Marmaris area. It is an ideal choice for eco-tourists, families with children (the educational aspect is huge) and anyone who wants to see something really special and is ready to follow simple rules for this. This is a beach that leaves not only pleasant memories of the holiday, but also a deep respect for nature.
