## Dalyan Beaches: Spotlights on Iztuzu

When speaking of beaches in Dalyan, people primarily mean one, but completely unique and world-famous location — Iztuzu Beach. The town of Dalyan itself does not have direct access to the sea, but this does not prevent it from being a center of attraction for beach holiday lovers.

### 1. [🐢 <u>**Iztuzu Beach — \"Turtle Beach\"**</u>](/dalyan/beach/Istuzu)

Iztuzu Beach is not just a place for swimming, but a real natural phenomenon and a crucial nature reserve.

- **Uniqueness:** This is a five-kilometer sand spit that serves as a natural barrier between the salty waters of the Mediterranean Sea and the fresh delta of the Dalyan River. You can take a dip in the sea, and then, after walking just a couple of minutes, rinse off in the cool fresh water of the river.
- **\"Turtle Beach\":** Iztuzu is one of the main nesting and egg-laying sites in the Mediterranean for the Loggerhead sea turtle species (Caretta Caretta). Because of this, the beach is under strict protection.
- **Rules of visit:** During the turtle breeding period (from May to October), the beach is closed to visitors at night (from 20:00 to 08:00). On the territory of the beach, it is forbidden to set up umbrellas and sunbeds in specially marked egg-laying zones. Pets are not allowed on the beach.
- **Infrastructure:** The beach has all the necessary infrastructure: cafes, showers, toilets, and changing cabins. The **Sea Turtle Research, Rescue and Rehabilitation Center (DEKAMER)** is also located here, where you can see rescued turtles and learn more about their protection.
- **How to get there:**
    - **By boat:** The most popular way. Cooperative boats regularly depart from the harbor in the center of Dalyan and sail along the scenic river right to the beach. The trip takes about 30-40 minutes.
    - **By dolmuş:** Minibuses depart from the town center and go to the beach along a scenic road overlooking Lake Sülüngür.

### Beaches in the Surroundings

Although Iztuzu is the main star, it is relatively easy to reach other beautiful beaches of the region from Dalyan.

#### 2. Sarigerme Beach (Sarıgerme)
Located about 30-40 minutes' drive from Dalyan, Sarigerme offers a wide and long beach with fine golden sand and a shallow entry into the sea, which is ideal for families with children. The beach is well-equipped and is part of a large tourist complex, but is accessible to everyone (entry may be paid).

#### 3. [🏝️ <u>**Aşı Cove (Aşı Koyu)**</u>](/dalyan/beach/asikoyu)
A hidden and less known cove, which can be reached by car on a dirt road. This is a place for those looking for privacy. A small pebble-sand beach is surrounded by cliffs, and the water here is crystal clear, making it a great place for snorkeling.
