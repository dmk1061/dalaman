## Dalyan Transport System: Traveling by River and Land

Dalyan is a town where the river is the main artery of life, and this is directly reflected in its transport system. Getting around here is a fascinating adventure in itself, combining land and water routes.

### 1. River Cooperative Boats (Dolmuş Tekne)

This is the main and most authentic form of transport in Dalyan, without which it is impossible to imagine a holiday in this town. Dozens of boats belonging to the local cooperative ply the river, taking tourists to the main attractions.

- **Routes:** The main and most popular route is **from the central pier of Dalyan to Iztuzu Beach**. Boats depart as they fill up throughout the day. The trip takes about 35-40 minutes and is a scenic excursion through a labyrinth of reed beds.
- **Other directions:** Boats are also the primary way to get to the mud baths on Lake Köyceğiz. In addition, you can hire a private boat for an individual excursion.
- **Crossing to Kaunos:** To get to the ruins of the ancient city of Kaunos, you need to use special rowing boats that will transfer you to the other side in a few minutes from the pier located opposite the famous Lycian tombs.

### 2. Dolmuşes (Minibuses)

Land transport in Dalyan is represented mainly by dolmuşes, which connect the town with the surrounding area.

- **Route to Iztuzu Beach:** Dolmuşes are an alternative to boats for a trip to the \"Turtle Beach\". They depart from the central square (near the mosque) and drive to the beach along a scenic road, skirting Lake Sülüngür.
- **Intercity routes:** From Dalyan, you can take a dolmuş to the town of Ortaca — the nearest transport hub. The trip takes about 15-20 minutes. From the Ortaca bus station, you can catch buses to larger cities such as Fethiye, Marmaris, Muğla, or Dalaman (airport).

### 3. Taxis

There are several taxi stands in the town. This is a convenient but more expensive way to get to your destination, especially relevant for trips to Dalaman Airport or neighboring cities if you are traveling in a group.

### 4. Vehicle Rental

- **Bicycles:** Dalyan is a small and fairly flat town, so a bicycle is an excellent way to explore the town itself and its immediate surroundings. There are several rental points in the center.
- **Cars and scooters:** Renting a car or scooter makes sense if you plan to actively explore the region outside Dalyan — for example, to head to Sarigerme Beach, Göcek, or further. Several rental agencies are located in the town center.

Thus, Dalyan's transport system allows you to easily combine different ways of getting around, turning ordinary trips into exciting journeys.
