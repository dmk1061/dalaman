Lake Sülüngür — Dalyan's Mirror Reflecting Sunsets
On the way to the famous Iztuzu Turtle Beach lies the quiet and mesmerizing Lake Sülüngür. This is not just a body of water, but an important part of the Dalyan River delta ecosystem. It was once part of the sea, but over time, river silt separated it, turning it into a brackish lake that became home to many birds and fish.

Main feature: Peace and sunsets
Unlike the dynamic sea, Sülüngür is a realm of silence. Its water surface, calm as a mirror, reflects the surrounding pine-covered hills and passing clouds.

But the main reason people come here is the sunsets. When the sun goes down, it colors the sky and water in incredible shades of orange, pink and purple. Watching this spectacle from the lake shore, in complete silence, is a truly meditative and unforgettable experience.

What to do here?
Nature observation: The lake is home to many species of birds, making it a great place for birdwatching.

Picnics: Along the shore there are several specially equipped areas with tables and benches where you can have a picnic.

Photography: This is a paradise for landscape photographers, especially during the "golden hour" before sunset.

Walking and cycling: There are trails around the lake, ideal for leisurely walking or cycling.

Important points
Swimming: Officially, swimming in the lake is not recommended or prohibited, as it is used by the local fish cooperative (DALKO) to breed mullet and sea bass (levrek).

Fishing: Amateur fishing from the shore is generally permitted, but it is also controlled by the cooperative.

Infrastructure: There are several simple restaurants and cafes on the shore where you can try fresh fish and enjoy the view.

Pros and cons
Pros:

Incredibly beautiful and peaceful landscapes.

Ideal place to watch the sunset.

Peace and quiet away from the tourist hustle and bustle.

Great place for a picnic and walks in nature.

Cons:

Limited options for swimming.

Transport is required to get here comfortably and spend time.

Who is Lake Sülüngür suitable for?
Sülüngür is a place for those who appreciate quiet, contemplative relaxation. It is ideal for romantics, photographers, nature lovers and anyone who wants to find a corner of peace after a noisy beach. It is a wonderful stop on the way to Iztuzu Beach or back to drink a cup of tea, admire the landscape and take some magnificent shots in memory of Dalyan's magical nature.
