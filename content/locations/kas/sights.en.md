Kaş is not only about the sea and beaches, but also a unique opportunity to touch ancient history. The ruins of the ancient city of Antiphellos are seamlessly woven into the modern development, and in the surrounding area there are many iconic sites of the Lycian civilization.

### Within the City

*   [🎭 <u>**Ancient Theater**</u>](/kas/sight/theater): Located on a hill west of the center, this Hellenistic theater is beautifully preserved and is used for concerts and events. From its top rows, there is a breathtaking view of the city, the sea, and the Greek island of Kastellorizo.

*   [🦁 <u>**Lion Tomb (Uzun Çarşı Mezarı)**</u>](/kas/sight/uzuncarsi): Right in the city center, on the main shopping street of Uzun Çarşı, stands a monumental Lycian sarcophagus from the 4th century BC. Its uniqueness lies in the combination of Lycian style with Hellenistic elements, as well as the relief carvings of lion heads.

*   [🪨 <u>**Rock Tombs**</u>](/kas/sight/kastombs): Above the city, on the mountain slopes, you can see Lycian tombs carved into the rock, which are beautifully illuminated at sunset.

### Boat Trips

*   [🌊 <u>**Sunken City of Kekova**</u>](/kas/sight/kekova): One of the most popular destinations for boat trips from Kaş. As a result of earthquakes, part of the ancient city of Dolichiste went underwater. Today, you can view the sunken ruins from a boat or kayak through the crystal-clear water. The tour also includes a visit to the village of Simena (Kaleköy) with its medieval fortress.

*   **Greek Island of Kastellorizo (Meis):** Located just 20 minutes by ferry from Kaş is the nearest Greek island. This is an excellent opportunity to make a day trip to another country, stroll around its picturesque harbor with colorful houses, and enjoy Greek cuisine.

### Ancient Cities in the Surroundings

Kaş is a convenient starting point for exploring the heritage of Lycia:

*   [👑 <u>**Xanthos**</u>](/kas/sight/xanthos): The ancient capital of Lycia, designated as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
*   [⛩️ <u>**Letoon**</u>](/kas/sight/letoon): The religious center of ancient Lycia, located near Xanthos.
*   [🏛️ <u>**Patara**</u>](/kas/sight/patara): A major port city with well-preserved ruins, including a theater and a triumphal arch, as well as the birthplace of Saint Nicholas.
