# Akçagerme Beach (Akçagerme Plajı) — Family-Friendly Getaway with a Water Park in Kaş

Akçagerme Beach is a shallow pebble beach located 4 km from Kaş, ideal for families due to its calm waters, water slides, and excellent municipal services.

Unlike the typically deep and rocky coves around Kaş, Akçagerme offers a safe and highly organized environment, making it the top choice for travelers with children.

### Beach Features and Student-Run Service
The beach sits in a sheltered bay that keeps the sea calm and free of large waves. The water depth increases very gradually, ensuring a safe swimming experience for young kids. The shoreline is covered in fine pebbles and holds the prestigious Blue Flag award for water cleanliness.

A standout feature of Akçagerme is its small on-site water park with waterslides and a dedicated pool. The beach is operated in partnership with the local vocational tourism school. As it serves as a training ground for students, the service is exceptionally polite and attentive, while the prices at the cafe are among the most budget-friendly in the [Kaş](/en/kas) region.

### Practical Information
- **Location:** [Kaş](/en/kas) region, along the Kaş-Kalkan highway, about 4 km west of the town center
- **Road:** Asphalt (directly off the main highway). Regular minibuses (dolmuş) run every 10–15 minutes from the center of Kaş.
- **Infrastructure:** Restaurant and cafe, toilets, showers, changing rooms, sunbed and umbrella rentals, children's waterslides, tennis and volleyball courts, wheelchair-accessible ramp, shaded picnic area
- **Entrance:** Free (sunbed rentals are paid but reasonably priced; parking is free)
- **Tip:** This is the most convenient beach in the Kaş area for families with toddlers. Arrive early during the summer months to secure sunbeds near the shoreline.
