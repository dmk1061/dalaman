Akçagerme – The Ideal Family Beach in Kaş with Its Own Water Park
Akçagerme Beach is a real find for families vacationing in Kaş. Unlike most beaches in this region, which are famous for their wild beauty and rocky shores, Akçagerme offers a completely different type of holiday: organized, safe, and highly oriented toward children.

Key Feature: Shallow Sea and Water Slides
Two things make this beach the top choice for families:

Shallow and calm sea: The bay is well protected, so there are almost never any waves here. Most importantly, the depth increases very slowly, allowing children to splash safely near the shore.

Mini-water park: Right on the beach, there is a small water park with several water slides and a separate pool. This is a real attraction for children, which allows parents to relax while their kids have fun.

Another unique detail: the beach is managed by students of the local vocational school of tourism and hotel management. This is their practical training, so the service is characterized by special diligence, and the prices in the cafe and for services are among the most budget-friendly in the region.

Beach and Atmosphere
Shoreline: This is a well-maintained beach covered with very fine pebbles, almost like coarse sand. The water is very clean, as confirmed by the "Blue Flag" award.

Atmosphere: Family-oriented, lively, but without loud music and the hustle of night clubs. During the day, there are many children, laughter, and fun.

Infrastructure: The beach is excellent equipped. It has everything: sun loungers, umbrellas, changing cabins, showers (with cool water from mountain springs), toilets. There are also a tennis court and a volleyball court.

How to Get There
The beach is located right off the main road leading from Kaş towards Kalkan/Fethiye. You can get there:

By minibus (dolmuş): Minibuses run regularly from the center of Kaş, and the trip takes about 10 minutes.

On foot: For walking enthusiasts, there is a scenic trail along the sea (about 4 km).

Pros and Cons
Pros:

An ideal place for holidays with children thanks to the shallow water and the water park.

Excellent infrastructure and very reasonable prices.

Clean and calm sea, marked with the "Blue Flag".

Easy transport accessibility from Kaş.

Cons:

It can be noisy and crowded due to the large number of families with children.

Not suitable for those looking for privacy and "wild" beauty.

The atmosphere is more reminiscent of an organized beach club than a secluded cove.

Who is Akçagerme suitable for?
This is the number one choice for everyone who came to Kaş with children. If your main goal is to provide children with a safe and fun beach holiday, then there is no better place in the vicinity of Kaş. It will also appeal to those who value comfort, full infrastructure, and dislike the rocky platform beaches characteristic of the center of Kaş.
