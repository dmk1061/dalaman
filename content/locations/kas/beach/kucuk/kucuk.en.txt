Küçük Çakıl – An Icy Oasis in the Heart of Kaş
Küçük Çakıl is a unique phenomenon. It is a tiny pebble bay, only a few meters wide, located a couple of minutes' walk from the main square of Kaş. This is a place where people come not to sunbathe for hours, but to get a quick and powerful boost of energy by dipping into crystal-clear and incredibly cold water.

Key Feature: Icy Spring Water
The secret of Küçük Çakıl lies in the cold underground springs that come to the surface right here, mixing with sea water. Because of this, the water temperature in the bay remains very low and invigorating even on the hottest summer day. For many, this is a real salvation from the heat. The water here is so transparent that you can see every single stone at the bottom.

Beach and Atmosphere
Shoreline: The public beach itself is a very small stretch of pebbles. The main space for relaxation is provided by the surrounding beach clubs and bars. They have built wooden platforms and terraces right on the rocks, equipping them with sun loungers, umbrellas, and ladders for descending into the water.

Atmosphere: Very lively, stylish, and urban. During the day, music plays, people drink cocktails and enjoy life. This is not a place for a quiet family holiday, but rather a trendy point of attraction for those who want to be in the center of the action.

Infrastructure
Beach clubs: To use a sun lounger and umbrella, you need to take a seat in one of the surrounding beach clubs (e.g., Derya Beach, Çınarlar Beach). As a rule, there is no fee for the lounger itself, but there is a "deposit" or a minimum spend required at the establishment's bar or restaurant.

Access: The tiny pebble section itself is public and free of charge.

Pros and Cons
Pros:

Location in the very center of the town, within walking distance.

Incredibly clean and refreshing (very cold) water.

Stylish, lively atmosphere with good music and cocktails.

Cons:

Very small and can be overcrowded.

The water is too cold for those who prefer warm sea water.

Relaxation is mostly paid (through the beach clubs).

Not suitable for families with small children.

Who is Küçük Çakıl suitable for?
Küçük Çakıl is an ideal place for those staying in the center of Kaş who want to take a quick dip in the sea without going far. It will appeal to young people and anyone who loves the lively atmosphere of beach clubs, good music, and is not afraid of icy water. If you are looking for a place to combine swimming with socializing and a couple of cocktails, Küçük Çakıl is your choice.
