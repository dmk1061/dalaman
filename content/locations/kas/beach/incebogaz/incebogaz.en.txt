İnceboğaz – The Beach with Two Seas
İnceboğaz is perhaps the most unusual beach in Kaş in terms of its geography. It is located on a narrow isthmus connecting the Çukurbağ Peninsula with the mainland. Its uniqueness lies in the fact that here, standing almost in the same spot, you can swim in two seas that are completely different in character.

Key Feature: Two Beaches in One
The road runs right along the ridge of the isthmus, and there are two beaches on either side of it:

Inner beach (overlooking the marina): This beach faces the sheltered and calm Bucak Bay (Bucak Koyu) and the Kaş Marina. The water here is almost always serene, like on a lake, and slightly warmer. This is an ideal place for families with young children and for those who do not like waves.

Outer beach (facing the open sea): This beach faces the open sea and the Greek island of Meis (Kastellorizo). The water here is generally cooler, and in windy weather, there may be small waves.

This duality allows you to choose a spot for swimming depending on the weather and your mood. If it is windy on one side, it will almost certainly be calm on the other.

Beach and Atmosphere
Shoreline: Both beaches are covered with fine pebbles. The entry into the water is shallow, making them convenient for everyone. The water, as everywhere in Kaş, is crystal clear.

Atmosphere: Very quiet and relaxed. It is a popular spot for locals and families who appreciate it for its safety and convenience.

Infrastructure: One of the beaches (usually the inner one) is managed by the municipality of Kaş. There is a cafe with affordable prices, sun lounger and umbrella rentals, showers, and toilets. The other beach often remains "wild," where you can come with your own towel.

Pros and Cons
Pros:

A unique opportunity to choose between two different beaches.

Calm and warm water on the inner side, ideal for children.

Cleanliness confirmed by the "Blue Flag".

Within walking distance (about 20-25 minutes) or a short transport ride from the center of Kaş.

Cons:

The beaches are quite narrow and can be crowded during the peak season.

The view of the marina on one side may not seem as "natural" as in other coves.

Who is İnceboğaz suitable for?
İnceboğaz is a versatile beach that will suit almost everyone. It is especially recommended for families with children because of its safe inner bay. It will also appeal to those who love tranquility but do not want to go far from the town. The ability to choose between two seas is a unique feature that makes İnceboğaz one of the most interesting and convenient beaches around Kaş.
