Limanağzı – A Boat Escape to a Paradise Bay
Limanağzı is not just a beach, but a whole bay with several beach clubs that can only be reached in one way – by water. This gives it a special charm and a sense of exclusivity. Just 15 minutes on a small dolmuş boat from the port of Kaş – and you are transported from a lively town to a world of peace, pure water, and untouched nature.

Key Feature: Accessible Only by Water and Long Daylight Hours
Sea taxi: The absence of a road makes Limanağzı a special place. Small boats run regularly from the central port, delivering holidaymakers to different points of the bay. The trip itself is already part of the adventure.

Sunny side: Thanks to its location, Limanağzı is the place where the sun shines the longest in the vicinity of Kaş. When the sun in the town itself already begins to set behind the mountains, you can still enjoy its warm rays here.

Beaches and Atmosphere
The bay is divided into several zones, each of which has its own beach club-restaurant. The most famous of them are Nuri's Beach, Bilal'in Yeri, La Moda Beach, and Delos Beach.

Shoreline: The beaches here are mostly pebbly, but some clubs have equipped convenient wooden platforms with sun loungers and ladders for descending into the water.

Atmosphere: Very relaxed, "barefoot," and close to nature. There is no loud music, only the sound of waves and cicadas. This is an ideal place for quiet swimming, snorkeling (the water is incredibly clear), and even encounters with Caretta Caretta sea turtles, which often swim into this quiet bay.

Infrastructure: Each beach club offers full service: sun loungers, umbrellas, delicious food, and drinks. Some of them also offer accommodation in cozy bungalows.

How to Get There
By dolmuş boat: Boats depart from the port of Kaş every half hour. You simply tell the captain which of the Limanağzı beaches you want to reach. The return boats run just as regularly until evening.

On foot: For hiking enthusiasts, a scenic section of the famous Lycian Way leads here. The trail starts from Büyük Çakıl beach and takes about 45-60 minutes.

Pros and Cons
Pros:

A sense of privacy and escape from civilization.

Crystal-clear and calm water, ideal for swimming and snorkeling.

Opportunity to see sea turtles.

Long daylight hours.

Authentic and relaxed atmosphere.

Cons:

It is required to pay for the boat to get there.

Relaxation is mostly paid (through the beach clubs).

Limited space, it can be crowded in peak season.

Who is Limanağzı suitable for?
Limanağzı is an ideal place for those who want to spend a day in a beautiful and quiet bay, enjoying nature and the purest sea. It is a wonderful choice for couples, small groups of friends, and anyone who wants to take a break from the city bustle without going far. If you are looking for a place with a "secret beach" atmosphere, but with all the amenities, Limanağzı will charm you.
