Büyük Çakıl – Cool Water by Day and Romance at Sunset
Büyük Çakıl Beach is a favorite spot for many locals and "in-the-know" tourists. It is much larger and more spacious than its "little brother" Küçük Çakıl and offers a more peaceful and natural holiday, while retaining the main highlight of Kaş – incredibly clean and cool water.

Key Feature: Cold Springs and Dinner on the Beach
Cool water: Just like in Küçük Çakıl, cold underground springs flowing from the mountains empty into the sea here. This makes the water very refreshing even at the peak of the summer heat. Because of this, the water in the bay is crystal clear and has a stunning turquoise hue.

Dinner at the water's edge: In the evening, real magic happens. Local restaurants located along the beach set up their tables and chairs right on the pebbles, right next to the water. Dining under the stars to the sound of the waves, with your feet resting on smooth stones, is one of the most romantic and authentic experiences Kaş has to offer.

Beach and Atmosphere
Shoreline: The beach is completely covered with smooth, rounded pebbles (hence the name). The entry into the water is rather steep, and the depth increases quickly.

Atmosphere: Much quieter and more relaxed than in the center. It is a place for slow relaxation, reading books, and enjoying nature. There is no loud music, only the sounds of nature and quiet conversations.

Infrastructure: Several family-run restaurants and guesthouses are located along the beach. During the day, they serve beachgoers. Usually, if you order food or drinks from them, sun loungers and umbrellas are provided free of charge.

Pros and Cons
Pros:

Very clean and refreshing water.

More spacious and less crowded than the beaches in the very center.

A unique opportunity to dine right on the beach by the water.

Within walking distance from the center of Kaş.

Cons:

A fully pebble beach, which requires water shoes.

Steep entry into the water and fast depth, which is not very suitable for small children.

The water may be too cold for some.

Who is Büyük Çakıl suitable for?
Büyük Çakıl is an ideal choice for those looking for a beautiful and clean beach within walking distance of the center of Kaş, but wanting to avoid the hustle and bustle. It is perfect for couples for a romantic dinner at sunset and for anyone who loves swimming in cool, crystal-clear water. This is a place with the real, non-touristy spirit of Kaş.
