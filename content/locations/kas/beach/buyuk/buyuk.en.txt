# Büyük Çakıl Beach (Büyük Çakıl Plajı) — Cool Springs and Seaside Dining in Kaş

Büyük Çakıl Beach is a wide pebble bay 1.5 km from Kaş center, famous for its cool freshwater springs and romantic dining experiences right on the shore.

Surrounded by lush green hills, this bay is recognized for having some of the clearest waters in the area, offering a more spacious alternative to the central town beach, Küçük Çakıl.

### Beach Features and Sunset Dinners
The hallmark of Büyük Çakıl is the presence of cold underground mountain springs flowing directly into the sea. This creates refreshing pockets of cool freshwater within the warm saltwater, making it a perfect spot to cool down during hot summer days. The beach is composed of smooth, rounded pebbles, and the sea floor drops off relatively fast, leading to deep waters close to the shore.

As evening approaches, the beach transforms. The local family-run restaurants clear the sunbeds and set up dining tables directly on the pebbled shore, just inches from the water. Dining by candlelight under the stars, with the gentle tide washing over the stones, is one of the most romantic and authentic experiences you can enjoy in [Kaş](/en/kas).

### Practical Information
- **Location:** [Kaş](/en/kas) region, eastern edge of the town, about 1.5–2 km from the center
- **Road:** Asphalt (a pleasant coastal road). Accessible on foot within 15–20 minutes, by taxi in 5 minutes, or via regular local minibuses (dolmuş) from the center.
- **Infrastructure:** Cafes, restaurants, toilets, showers, changing rooms, sunbed and umbrella rentals
- **Entrance:** Free (sunbeds are usually included if you order food or drinks from the beachfront cafes, or can be rented separately)
- **Tip:** Bring water shoes due to the pebbly entry into the water. If you plan to dine by the water during the busy summer months, booking a table in advance is highly recommended.
