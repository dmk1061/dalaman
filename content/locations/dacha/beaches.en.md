# Datça Beaches and Bays

**The Datça Peninsula is famous for its jagged coastline and features more than 50 bays and beaches. Here you can find options for every taste: from long sandy beaches with a gentle slope to secluded pebbly bays hidden among the cliffs. The water here is crystal clear and has a stunning turquoise hue.**

---

### Beaches on the Southern Coast (Mediterranean Sea)

The water here is usually calmer and warmer.

-   [🏖️ <u>**Taşlık (Taşlık Plajı)**</u>](/dacha/beach/Taslik): The town beach in the center of Datça, right next to the harbor. Pebbly, organized, and with many cafes and restaurants nearby. The water here is believed to have healing properties due to freshwater springs flowing into the sea.
-   [🏖️ <u>**Kumluk (Kumluk Plajı)**</u>](/dacha/beach/Kumluk): Another central beach, but sandy. Very popular with families with children because of the shallow water.
-   [🏊‍♂️ <u>**Kargı (Kargı Koyu)**</u>](/dacha/beach/Kargi): A picturesque and highly popular bay 3 km from the center. Pebbly beach, crystal clear water, and several cozy restaurants. An ideal place to spend the whole day.
-   [🏖️ <u>**Palamutbükü (Palamutbükü)**</u>](/dacha/beach/Palamutbuku): One of the most famous and beautiful bays on the peninsula, with a long (2 km) sand-and-pebble beach. Along the promenade, there are many hotels, guesthouses, and fish restaurants.
-   [🌅 <u>**Ovabükü (Ovabükü)**</u>](/dacha/beach/Ovobuku) and Hayıtbükü (Hayıtbükü): Two neighboring, quieter and more "rural" bays with beautiful beaches and several family guesthouses. Ideal for a peaceful holiday.

---

### Beaches on the Northern Coast (Aegean Sea)

Here, the water is slightly cooler and the wind often creates small waves, which delights windsurfing enthusiasts.

-   **Akyar (Akyar Koyu):** Known for its snow-white cliffs and incredible water color. The beach is wild, without amenities.
-   **Çiftebük (Çiftebük Koyu):** A beautiful bay popular with camping enthusiasts.
-   **Gereme (Gereme Koyu):** Another wild and very beautiful bay with pristine water.

---

### Wild Bays and Boat Tours

The best way to explore the Datça coastline is to go on a **one-day boat tour**. Every day, dozens of boats depart from the Datça harbor, visiting the most beautiful bays that are difficult to access by land.

-   **What you will see:** A typical route includes visits to bays where you can go snorkeling, swim in the clearest water, and sunbathe away from the crowds.
-   **Knidos:** Many tours include a stop at the ruins of the ancient city of Knidos, allowing you to combine a beach holiday with a cultural program.
-   [🏝️ <u>**Domuzbükü (Domuzbükü - "Pig Bay")**</u>](/dacha/beach/Domuzbuku): One of the most famous "wild" bays, accessible only by sea.
