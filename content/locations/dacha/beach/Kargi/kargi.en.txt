Kargı Bay — A natural refuge near the town
Just a few kilometers from the busy center of Datça lies a place that feels like another world—the picturesque Kargı Bay (Kargı Koyu). This is an ideal destination for those who want to escape the city's hustle and bustle and immerse themselves in nature without traveling too far.

Feature: Shelter and tranquility
The main advantage of Kargı Bay is its geographical position. It is surrounded by hills that create a natural barrier against northern winds. Thanks to this, the sea here is almost always calm, smooth, and crystal clear, and the water is slightly warmer than on open beaches. This creates a sense of privacy and peace, even when the beach has many visitors.

Beach and nature
Coastline: The beach is covered with large, smooth, multi-colored pebbles. Walking on them is a pleasure, and the water remains incredibly transparent thanks to the stones.

Water: The sea here has a stunning turquoise hue. The depth increases rather gradually, which is convenient for swimming.

Surroundings: Hills covered with olive and pine trees create a magnificent backdrop and fill the air with healing scents. Previously, numerous windmills were located here, and the ruins of one can still be seen on a hill.

Infrastructure and activities
How to get there: The bay can be reached by car (about 3 km from the center of Datça) or on foot along a scenic path in about 40-50 minutes. Local minibus-dolmuşes also run here.

Amenities: In the bay, there are several beach clubs and restaurants where you can rent sunbeds and umbrellas, as well as have a delicious lunch. Prices here may be slightly higher than in the town. There are also public free sections of the beach.

Activities: Kargı is an ideal place for peaceful swimming, snorkeling (especially along the rocky edges of the bay), and simply relaxing in nature.

Pros and Cons
Pros:
- Incredible natural beauty and peaceful atmosphere.
- Protection from the wind and always calm sea.
- Crystal clear, warm water.
- Proximity to the town while maintaining a sense of complete isolation.

Cons:
- Pebble beach: Special shoes may be required.
- Popularity: In the peak of the season and on weekends, it can be quite crowded.
- Limited infrastructure: The choice of establishments is smaller than in the town center.

Who is Kargı Bay for?
Kargı is the choice of those who look for "wild" beauty but with access to basic comfort. This is a place for lovers of nature, clean sea, and peaceful rest. If you want to spend the day away from noise, enjoying swimming in a picturesque bay and lunch with a view of the turquoise water, Kargı will not disappoint you. It is the perfect compromise between a wild beach and a developed urban holiday.
