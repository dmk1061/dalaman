# Taşlık Beach (Taşlık Plajı) — Where the Warm Sea Meets the Cool Mineral Spring

Taşlık Beach is a popular pebble-and-sand city beach in Datça, famous for its proximity to the mineral-rich, therapeutic waters of the Ilıca Pond.

Located within walking distance of the central harbor, this public beach is beloved for a unique natural phenomenon where cool freshwater streams mix with the warm salt waters of the Aegean Sea.

### Beach Features and the Ilıca Spring
The highlight of Taşlık Beach is the small freshwater Ilıca Pond (Ilıca Göleti) sitting just behind the shoreline. Fed by cool underground springs, the pond’s water is slightly mineralized and contains sulfur, giving it a therapeutic reputation. As it flows through the gravel into the sea, it creates refreshing, cool pockets of freshwater within the warm seawater, offering a pleasant contrasting temperature experience.

The shore is composed of smooth pebbles of small to medium size, which keeps the water crystal clear. A scenic promenade runs along the beach, leading directly to the harbor and the nearby Kent Park, which offers green spaces and playgrounds for children.

### Practical Information
- **Location:** Central [Datça](/en/dacha), just south of the harbor
- **Road:** Asphalt (easily reachable on foot from the town center)
- **Infrastructure:** Changing cabins, showers, toilets, sunbed and umbrella rentals, nearby cafes, restaurants, and a park
- **Entrance:** Free (sunbed rental is paid)
- **Tip:** Take a dip in the natural pool of the Ilıca Pond after swimming in the sea. Wearing water shoes is recommended due to the pebbly shore.
