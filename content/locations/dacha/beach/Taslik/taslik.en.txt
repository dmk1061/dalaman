Taşlık Beach in Datça: Where a cool lake meets the warm sea
In the very heart of the picturesque town of Datça, on the peninsula of the same name in Turkey, lies one of the most unusual beaches loved by locals—Taşlık (Taşlık Plajı). Its name translates as "stony," which honestly describes its pebbly shore, but the main value of this place lies not in the stones, but in a unique natural phenomenon.

Main feature: The merging of two waters
Right behind the beach strip is the small freshwater Lake Ilıca (Ilıca Göleti), which is fed by cold mountain streams. The water in the lake is slightly mineralized and has a reputation for being healing due to a small sulfur content. This cool freshwater stream from the lake flows through the pebbles and empties directly into the warm, salty waters of the Aegean Sea.

It is this mixing that creates a unique effect: swimming in the sea, you will feel how the warm marine currents are replaced by invigorating and cool freshwater "tongues." This natural contrast shower not only refreshes in the summer heat, but, as many believe, has a beneficial effect on health.

The beach itself and infrastructure
Taşlık is a public beach with crystal clear, transparent water. The shore is covered with smooth pebbles ranging from small to medium size, so the water here is never turbid.

Location: The beach is located within walking distance of the center of Datça and the picturesque yacht harbor.

Amenities: Here you can rent sunbeds and umbrellas at a reasonable price. Public showers and changing cabins are also available.

Food and drinks: Along the promenade, there are many cafes, restaurants, and bars where you can have a snack or a full lunch with a sea view.

What to do?
Swimming: Enjoy the unusual sensations of mixing warm and cold water.

Relaxation: Sunbathe and relax, admiring the views of the bay and passing yachts.

Walks: The beach is part of a beautiful promenade, which is ideal for evening walks to the port of Datça.

Pros and Cons
Pros:
- Unique natural phenomenon: Mixing of fresh and salt water.
- Central location: Easy to get to, all infrastructure is at hand.
- Very clean water: Thanks to the pebbles.
- Picturesque views.

Cons:
- Pebbly shore: It can be uncomfortable for those used to sand (special water shoes are recommended).
- Crowded: In the peak of the season, the beach can be quite busy due to its popularity and central location.
- Cool water: To some, the water may seem too invigorating even in the heat.

Who is Taşlık Beach for?
This beach is an ideal choice for those who appreciate natural highlights, love clear water, and are not afraid of pebbles. It is perfect for travelers staying in the town of Datça who want to have a good beach within walking distance, combining relaxation with walks and visits to cafes. Families with small children who want to build sandcastles might want to look for other beaches on the peninsula.

Summary: Taşlık is not just a beach, but a real landmark of Datça. This is a place where you can fully feel the unique character of this amazing corner of Turkey.
