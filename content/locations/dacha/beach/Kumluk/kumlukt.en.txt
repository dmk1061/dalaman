Kumluk Beach — The soul of evening Datça
If Taşlık Beach is a place for an invigorating morning swim, then Kumluk (Kumluk Plajı) is, without a doubt, the heart and soul of Datça's evening life. Its name translates as "sandy," and it fully lives up to this name, offering guests soft golden sand, which is a rarity for this region.

By day a beach, by night a restaurant under the stars
Kumluk has a unique double life.

By day: It is a classic town beach. The sea here is very shallow and calm, making it an ideal place for families with children. The water warms up perfectly, and the gentle slope into the sea ensures safety for the youngest swimmers. During the day, you can rent sunbeds and umbrellas here and enjoy a relaxed beach holiday.

By night: True magic happens. As soon as the sun begins to set, the staff of coastal restaurants remove the sunbeds and set up tables right on the sand, at the very edge of the water. The beach turns into a huge open-air restaurant. Having dinner on Kumluk Beach, with your feet buried in the warm sand and a view of the illuminated bay and yachts before your eyes, is one of the most romantic and memorable experiences in Datça.

Infrastructure and atmosphere
Location: The beach is located in the very center of the town, in a cozy bay sheltered from the winds.

Restaurants: Kumluk is famous for its fish restaurants and meyhanes (traditional Turkish taverns). They serve the freshest seafood, delicious mezes (appetizers), and Aegean cuisine.

Atmosphere: In the evening, there is a lively, yet very cozy and relaxed atmosphere. The sounds of live music, laughter, clinking glasses, and the sound of the surf mix into a unique cocktail of a southern evening.

Pros and Cons
Pros:
- Sandy shore and shallow sea: Ideal for families with children.
- Unique evening atmosphere: Opportunity to dine right on the beach by the water.
- Central location: Easy access and proximity to all town infrastructure.
- Wide choice of excellent restaurants.

Cons:
- Crowded: Like any popular town beach, it can be overcrowded in high season.
- Water quality: Due to the sandy bottom and the large number of people, the water may not be as crystal clear as on pebble beaches.
- Prices: Dinner at restaurants on the beach can be more expensive than in establishments slightly further away.

Who is Kumluk Beach for?
Kumluk is a place for those who look for a comfortable sandy beach by day and a romantic, lively atmosphere by night. It is a must-visit for gourmets and anyone wishing to feel the true spirit of an Aegean resort town. If you want to combine a beach holiday with gastronomic pleasures and evening walks, Kumluk will become your favorite spot in Datça.
