# Kumluk Beach (Kumluk Plajı) — The Sandy Heart of Datça's Evening Life

Kumluk Beach is a popular sandy stretch in the center of Datça, known for its shallow waters and romantic seaside dining where tables are set right on the sand.

Unlike most rocky bays in the area, Kumluk offers soft golden sand and calm, shallow waters, making it a favorite for both families and couples.

### Day and Night Experience
During the day, Kumluk functions as a classic city beach. The water is exceptionally shallow and warm, providing a safe swimming environment for young children. You can easily rent sunbeds and umbrellas from local establishments or relax in the free areas.

At sunset, the beach undergoes a magical transformation. Beachfront restaurants clear away the sunbeds and set up candlelit tables directly on the sand. Dining with your feet in the warm sand, watching the reflection of the town lights on the calm bay, is one of the most memorable experiences in [Datça](/en/dacha).

### Practical Information
- **Location:** Central [Datça](/en/dacha), along the Sevgi Yolu pedestrian promenade
- **Road:** Asphalt (easily accessible on foot or by car within the town)
- **Infrastructure:** Changing cabins, showers, toilets, sunbed and umbrella rentals, numerous cafes and restaurants
- **Entrance:** Free (sunbed rentals or restaurant orders are paid)
- **Tip:** Visit during the day if you are traveling with children, and make sure to book a table directly on the sand for an unforgettable dinner at dusk.
