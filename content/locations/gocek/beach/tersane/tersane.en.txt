Tersane Island — A Historic Harbor in the Heart of Gocek Bay
Tersane Island is the largest island in the famous Gocek Bay. Its name, which means "Shipyard Island," speaks for itself: for centuries, since the Ottoman Empire, it served as an important shipbuilding and ship repair center. Today, it is one of the most popular and picturesque stops for yachts and tour boats.

Main Feature: Two Bays and Historical Ruins
Summer and Winter Harbors: The island has two main bays that cut deeply into the land. The main one, known as the Winter Harbor, is so well protected from the winds that it served as a safe haven for ships even in stormy weather. The second, more open bay, is called the Summer Harbor.

Traces of the Past: Until 1923, a Greek village flourished on the island. After the population exchange, the inhabitants left their homes. Today, walking around the island, you can see the ruins of their stone houses, an old watchtower, and the remains of the very shipyard that gave the island its name. These ruins, overgrown with grass and flowers, create a very picturesque and slightly melancholy atmosphere.

Beach and Atmosphere
Coastline: There is a small pebble beach in the main bay, but mostly people swim directly from yachts and boats in the crystal-clear, calm water.

Atmosphere: A very popular spot, especially during the day when dozens of tour boats arrive. In the bay, there is one restaurant with a jetty, which is the center of all activity. It is a lively place where you can enjoy a delicious lunch of fresh fish and watch the life of yachtsmen.

Infrastructure: Limited to one restaurant and its jetty. There are no hotels or shops on the island; it is intended mainly for day visits.

Pros and Cons
Pros:

A very beautiful and well-protected bay, ideal for swimming.
An interesting combination of natural beauty and historical ruins.
A mandatory and one of the best stops on the "12 Islands" tour.
An opportunity to connect with the history of the region's Greek population.

Cons:

Very crowded during the day.
Accessible only by water.
Limited infrastructure.

Who is Tersane Island for?
Visiting Shipyard Island is an essential part of any boat trip in Gocek Bay. It will appeal to everyone who loves beautiful scenery, clean seas, and is interested in history. It is a wonderful place to stop for lunch, swim in calm waters, and take a short walk through the ruins of the old Greek village, imagining how life bustled here a hundred years ago.
