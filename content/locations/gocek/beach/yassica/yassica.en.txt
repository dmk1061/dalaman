Yassica Islands — A Natural Water Park in Gocek Bay
The Yassica Islands (Flat Islands) are not just a stop on a boat tour, but a real natural playground. This is a group of several tiny, uninhabited islets located so close to each other that they give the impression of a single entity. The place is famous for its shallow, safe waters and is an absolute favorite among families with children.

Main Feature: Shallows and the Ability to Walk Between Islands
The uniqueness of the Yassica Islands lies in the shallows that form between the islets.

Natural pool for children: The water here is so shallow that even the smallest children can safely stand and play in the water. This creates the feeling of a huge natural pool filled with sea water.

Walking on water: In some places, you can literally walk from one island to another along a submerged sandy spit.

"Devil's Pool": On one of the islets, there is a small, jacuzzi-like natural pool that is very popular for photos.

Beach and Atmosphere
Coastline: On the largest of the islets, there is a small but very picturesque crescent-shaped sandy beach. The other islets are mostly rocky, covered with pines and olive trees.

Atmosphere: Very fun, lively, and family-oriented. During the day, many tour boats dock here at the same time, and the air is filled with laughter and joyful shouts. This place is made for fun and carefree swimming.

Infrastructure: There is no permanent infrastructure on the islands. All amenities (toilets, food, drinks) are onboard your tour boat. Sometimes during the season, locals pull up in small boats to sell gözleme or ice cream.

Pros and Cons
Pros:

The ideal and safest spot for families with children.
Very beautiful and unusual geography.
Opportunity to swim in shallow, warm, and calm water.
Fun and lively atmosphere.

Cons:

Very crowded due to the large number of tour boats.
Accessible only by water.
Complete lack of infrastructure on the islands themselves.

Who are the Yassica Islands for?
This is a must-visit stop for anyone taking the "12 Islands" tour from Fethiye or Gocek, especially for families with children. If you want to see a place where your children can splash around in the sea in absolute safety while you enjoy stunning views, the Yassica Islands will be the brightest and most fun moment of your boat trip.
