# Inlice Beach (İnlice Plajı) — Spacious Municipal Beach near Göcek

Inlice Beach is a scenic municipal beach with dark volcanic sand and a pine grove, located along the D400 highway just minutes away from Göcek.

Since the yachting port of [Göcek](/en/gocek) itself lacks accessible, free-to-use beaches within town limits, Inlice serves as the primary public beach for locals and tourists. The coastline is covered with dark volcanic sand mixed with small pebbles near the water. The sea is open and clean, with depth increasing rapidly, and mild waves often develop in the afternoon.

The beach is managed by the Fethiye Municipality (Fethiye Belediyesi), ensuring cleanliness and reasonable prices. Amenities include a budget-friendly municipal cafe, changing cabins, free showers, toilets, and sunbed/umbrella rentals. Directly behind the beach, under the shade of a pine grove, is a designated picnic area (Mesire Alanı).

### Practical Information
- **Location:** 6 km east of [Göcek](/en/gocek) towards [Fethiye](/en/fethiye) (approx. 10 minutes by car)
- **Road:** Paved asphalt road D400 with a short access road to the coast
- **Infrastructure:** Municipal cafe, toilets, showers, changing cabins, sunbeds and umbrellas for rent, lifeguard station, pine-shaded picnic area
- **Entrance:** Free for pedestrians; a nominal municipal fee is charged for car parking
- **Tip:** The dark sand heats up quickly under the sun, so make sure to bring flip-flops or water shoes. Keep an eye on children while swimming, as the water gets deep close to the shore.
