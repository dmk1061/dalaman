Inlice Beach — Affordable Relaxation Neighboring Luxurious Gocek
Inlice Beach is a popular spot and highly loved by locals, located in a picturesque bay just a few kilometers from the elite resort of Gocek. While Gocek itself is geared towards yachtsmen and lacks its own large beaches, Inlice serves as an excellent "public" beach for it, offering a spacious shore, clean water, and great infrastructure at affordable prices.

Main Feature: Space and Picnic Area
Wide coastline: The beach is quite long and wide, so it never feels crowded. You can always find a comfortable spot to settle down.

Picnic Area (Mesire Alanı): Right behind the beach, a large, shady area with pine and eucalyptus trees begins, equipped with picnic tables and barbecue spots. This makes Inlice incredibly popular for all-day family outings, especially on weekends.

Beach and Atmosphere
Coastline: The shore is covered with dark, dense sand, mixed in some places with fine pebbles. The sea here is clean, but unlike enclosed bays, it can be slightly wavy. The depth increases unevenly: there are both shallow areas and places where it becomes deep rather quickly.

Atmosphere: Very democratic, family-friendly, and relaxed. This is not a trendy beach club, but a classic public beach where people come to relax, swim, and spend time with their family.

Infrastructure: The beach is well-equipped by the municipality. Entrance to the beach itself is free (a small fee may be charged for parking). It has everything you need:

A large cafe with very affordable prices for food and drinks.

Sun lounger and umbrella rentals.

Free showers, toilets, and changing cabins.

Pros and Cons
Pros:

Spacious and not too crowded beach.
Great picnic and barbecue area in the shade of the trees.
Good infrastructure and very affordable prices.
Easy transport accessibility from the main Fethiye-Dalaman highway.

Cons:

Dark sand that heats up significantly in the sun.
The sea is not always perfectly calm; there can be waves.
Uneven depth requires attention when relaxing with small children.

Who is Inlice Beach for?
Inlice is an ideal choice for those looking for a simple, inexpensive, and comfortable beach holiday. It is perfect for families and large groups who want to combine swimming in the sea with a picnic in nature. It is an excellent "people's" beach that serves as a wonderful alternative to the expensive and private beach clubs of Gocek, offering everything necessary for a great day by the sea.
