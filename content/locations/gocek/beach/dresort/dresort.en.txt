# D-Resort Beach (Blue Point Beach) — Luxury Beach Club with White Sand in Gocek

D-Resort Beach is the only sandy beach club in the center of Gocek, boasting soft white sand, premium facilities, and first-class services for guests.

### Premium Beach Experience
This exclusive beach belongs to the five-star D-Resort Gocek hotel and is located within the high-end D-Marin marina complex. The soft, imported white sand does not get hot under the sun. Tucked into a sheltered bay, the sea is always calm, warm, and free of waves. Guests can relax on wide wooden decks with comfortable sunbeds and umbrellas, or book private luxury cabanas for ultimate privacy. The beachfront restaurant and bar, The Breeze, serves fine dining and drinks directly to your sunbed.

### Access Details
For guests staying at D-Resort Gocek, entry is free, and the hotel provides shuttle service via golf carts. Non-hotel guests can access the beach by purchasing a daily Day Pass at a premium rate. The pass includes the use of a sunbed, umbrella, beach towel, and access to all facilities.

### Practical Information
* **Location:** [Gocek](/en/gocek), D-Marin marina area (within walking distance from the town center).
* **Road:** Asphalt.
* **Infrastructure:** Premium sunbeds, umbrellas, private cabanas, towels, showers, toilets, changing rooms, restaurant and bar (The Breeze), parking.
* **Entrance:** Free for D-Resort Gocek hotel guests; paid for non-residents (Day Pass at a premium rate).
* **Tip:** If you are not staying at the hotel, it is highly recommended to call the hotel reception before visiting. During the peak summer season or days with high hotel occupancy, entry for non-residents may be restricted.
