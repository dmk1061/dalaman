Thanks to its location near an international airport and the main coastal highway, Göcek has excellent transport accessibility. Getting around the town itself and its surroundings is also very convenient.

### **How to Get to Göcek**

#### **By Plane**
The nearest airport is **Dalaman International Airport (DLM)**, which is located just 22 km from Göcek. The car trip takes about 20-25 minutes.
*   **From the Airport:** You can take a taxi, order a private transfer, or use the **Havaş/MUTTAŞ** shuttle buses heading towards Fethiye (ask the driver to stop in Göcek).

#### **By Car**
Göcek is located directly on the main highway **D400**, which connects all the resorts on the coast. The road from Fethiye (30 km) or Dalaman (22 km) is very scenic and simple. To enter the town from the Dalaman side, you can use the paid toll car tunnel, which shortens the way.

#### **By Bus**
All intercity buses running on the Fethiye-Muğla route pass through Göcek. Also, local minibuses (dolmuşes) regularly run between Göcek and Fethiye.

### **Getting Around Göcek and Surroundings**

#### **On Foot**
Göcek is a very compact and cozy town. The entire central part, the promenade, and the marinas are easy to explore on foot, enjoying the views and the atmosphere.

#### **Dolmuşes (Minibuses)**
This is the main type of local public transport. Dolmuşes regularly depart from the center of Göcek to neighboring **Fethiye**, as well as to the municipal beach **Inlice**.

#### **Taxi**
Taxi stands are located in the center of the town. This is a convenient way to get to nearby beaches or neighboring villages if you are traveling in a group.

#### **Water Transport**
This is Göcek's trademark.
*   **Water Taxi:** Will take you to nearby beaches and islands inaccessible by land.
*   **Excursion Boats:** Group "12 Islands" tours depart daily from the port.
*   **Private Boat Rental:** The ideal option for those who want to explore the gulf at their own pace. You can rent a boat with a captain for the whole day.
