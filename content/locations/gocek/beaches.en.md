Göcek, as an elite yachting resort, offers a unique beach holiday, which is mainly related to the sea and islands. There are some great options in the town itself, but the real gems are hidden in the picturesque bays of the gulf.

### **Beaches in Town and Nearby**

*   [🏨 <u>**D-Resort Beach at D-Marin**</u>](/gocek/beach/dresort):
    *   **Description:** This is a luxurious private beach with white sand specially imported to create the perfect atmosphere. The beach belongs to the D-Resort hotel but is open to anyone for an additional fee.
    *   **Infrastructure:** Offers a full range of services: comfortable sun loungers, umbrellas, an elite restaurant, and impeccable service. An ideal place for a comfortable and respectable holiday.

*   [🏖️ <u>**Inlice Municipal Beach (İnlice Halk Plajı)**</u>](/gocek/beach/inlice):
    *   **Description:** Located about 5 km from the center of Göcek, this public beach is a great alternative to private clubs. It is covered with dark sand and has a gentle entry into the water.
    *   **How to Get There:** You can easily reach the beach by dolmuş (shared minibus) which runs regularly from the town center.
    *   **Infrastructure:** There is a cafe, showers, toilets on the beach, and you can rent sun loungers and umbrellas at affordable prices.

### **Coves and Islands: "12 Islands" Tour**

The main charm of a beach holiday in Göcek is the opportunity to explore numerous secluded bays and islands by boat. Excursions known as the **"12 Islands" tour** depart daily from the port.

*   [👑 <u>**Hamam Bay (Hamam Koyu) / Cleopatra's Baths**</u>](/gocek/beach/hamam):
    *   A very popular stop, famous for its submerged ruins of Roman baths. Legend has it that Cleopatra herself bathed here. The water in the bay is crystal clear and has a deep turquoise hue.

*   [🏛️ <u>**Tersane Island (Tersane Adası)**</u>](/gocek/beach/tersane):
    *   The largest island in the Göcek Gulf. Here you can not only swim but also see the ruins of an old Greek village and a Byzantine shipyard.

*   [🏝️ <u>**Yassıca Islands (Yassıca Adaları)**</u>](/gocek/beach/yassica):
    *   A group of several small islands connected by sandy spits. The shallow water between the islands creates a natural pool ideal for swimming.

*   [🎨 <u>**Bedri Rahmi Bay (Bedri Rahmi Koyu)**</u>](/gocek/beach/bedri):
    *   Named after the famous Turkish painter who drew a fish on one of the rocks during a trip. This is a picturesque place with clear water surrounded by a pine forest.

**Tip:** Renting a small private boat for the day will allow you to plan your own itinerary and visit the most secluded and beautiful corners of the bay at your own pace.
