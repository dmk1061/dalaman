Getting to Köyceğiz and moving around its picturesque surroundings is quite simple thanks to a well-developed transport network, which includes air, bus, and water connections.

### **How to Get to Köyceğiz**

#### **By Plane**
The nearest airport to Köyceğiz is **Dalaman International Airport (DLM)**. It is located just 30-35 kilometers from the town, making air travel one of the most convenient ways to reach this resort.
*   **From Dalaman Airport:** Upon arrival, you can take a taxi, book a private transfer, or use the bus services of Havaş or MUTTAŞ, which run to the Fethiye or Marmaris bus terminals, stopping at the junction to Köyceğiz. From there, you can easily reach the town center by local dolmuş.

#### **By Bus**
Köyceğiz has excellent bus connections with major cities in Turkey.
*   **Intercity Buses:** Companies like **Pamukkale Turizm**, **Kamil Koç**, and **Metro Turizm** run regular services to Muğla, Fethiye, Marmaris, and Antalya, where you can make a convenient transfer to local transport to Köyceğiz.
*   **Local Buses (Dolmuşes):** The main regional carrier is the **Batı Antalya** cooperative. Dolmuşes run regularly on the Fethiye-Muğla route, passing through Köyceğiz. This is the most economical and popular way to travel between coastal towns.

### **Transport in Köyceğiz and Around**

#### **Dolmuşes and Local Buses**
The dolmuş network is the backbone of public transport in the region. They connect Köyceğiz with neighboring towns and villages, such as Dalyan, Ortaca, Sultaniye, and others. The stops are usually located in the town center, but you can also stop a dolmuş simply by waving your hand on the road.

#### **Taxis**
Taxis are easy to find in the center of Köyceğiz, especially near the bus station. This is a comfortable, though more expensive, way to reach specific places where public transport does not run, or for travel at night.

#### **Water Transport**
Given its location on the lake shore, water transport plays an important role in Köyceğiz.
*   **Boat Cooperatives:** Boat cooperatives operate in Köyceğiz and neighboring Dalyan, offering regular runs and individual tours. By boat, you can reach:
    *   Sultaniye Hot Springs
    *   Ancient City of Kaunos
    *   Iztuzu Beach
    *   Various bays and islands on the lake.

#### **Car or Scooter Rental**
For maximum freedom of movement and exploring the remote corners of the region, you can rent a car or scooter. There are several local and international rental companies in Köyceğiz and at Dalaman Airport. Having your own transport will allow you to visit places like Yuvarlakçay Gorge at your own pace and explore lesser-known beaches and villages.
