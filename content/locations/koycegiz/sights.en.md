Köyceğiz and its surroundings are rich in both natural and historical sights that attract tourists from all over the world. This region offers a unique combination of ancient history, picturesque landscapes, and opportunities for wellness and relaxation.

### [🌊 <u>**Köyceğiz Lake (Köyceğiz Gölü)**</u>](/koycegiz/sight/Seafront)

This is the main jewel of the region. A huge freshwater lake of tectonic origin, connected to the sea by the Dalyan River.
*   **What to do:** Take a boat trip on the lake to enjoy panoramic views of the mountains covered with pine forests. Do some birdwatching, as the lake is an important bird sanctuary. Visit the small Hapisane Island, also known as "Prison Island," featuring the ruins of a Byzantine monastery.

### **Dalyan River and Delta**

A picturesque river flowing from Köyceğiz Lake to the Mediterranean Sea. A boat trip along this river is a must-do for every tourist.
*   **What to do:** During the boat excursion, you will see the famous Lycian rock tombs carved into the cliffs, sail through labyrinths of reed beds, and enjoy the incredible natural beauty of the delta.

### [🏛️ <u>**Ancient City of Kaunos (Kaunos)**</u>](/koycegiz/sight/Kaunos)

On the bank of the Dalyan River, opposite the modern town of Dalyan, lie the ruins of the ancient Carian city of Kaunos, with a history spanning over 3,000 years.
*   **What to see:**
    *   **Lycian Rock Tombs:** Majestic tombs of the kings of Kaunos, carved high into the cliffs, which serve as the region's trademark.
    *   **Acropolis:** Climb to the top of the hill to see the remains of the fortress walls and enjoy a breathtaking view of the river, delta, and Iztuzu Beach.
    *   **Amphitheater:** A well-preserved ancient theater that still impresses with its acoustics.
    *   **Roman Baths, Agora, and Temples:** Walk through the ruins to imagine life in the ancient port city.

### [♨️ <u>**Sultaniye Hot Springs and Mud Baths (Sultaniye Kaplıcaları)**</u>](/koycegiz/sight/Sultanye)

Located on the shores of Köyceğiz Lake, these springs have been famous for their healing properties since the Roman Empire.
*   **What to do:** Take a mud bath, which is believed to rejuvenate the skin, and then plunge into a pool with thermal water rich in sulfur and other minerals. This is not only beneficial for health but also very fun.

### **Iztuzu Beach (İztuzu Plajı)**

A unique sandy spit about 4.5 km long that separates the Dalyan River delta from the Mediterranean Sea.
*   **What to do:** Relax on the pristine sand, swim in the sea on one side of the beach and in the fresh river on the other. The beach is one of the world's most important nesting sites for Caretta caretta sea turtles. Visit the Sea Turtle Rescue and Rehabilitation Center (DEKAMER) to learn more about these amazing creatures.

### [💧 <u>**Yuvarlakçay Waterfall (Yuvarlakçay)**</u>](/koycegiz/sight/Youvarlakcay)

A few kilometers from Köyceğiz lies this picturesque gorge with a crystal clear and very cold river.
*   **What to do:** The perfect place to escape the summer heat. Swim in the ice-cold water, swing on swings suspended directly over the river, and have lunch at one of the numerous fish restaurants on the banks.
