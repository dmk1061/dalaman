Sultaniye Beach — A contrast shower from nature
The beach at the Sultaniye hot springs offers one of the most unusual swimming experiences in all of Turkey. This is not a place for lazy lying on the sand. People come here for a unique combination of relaxation in hot mineral waters and a bracing plunge into the cool freshwater lake, all surrounded by stunning mountain scenery.

Main Feature: Hot and Cold
The heart of this place is the Sultaniye hot springs, famous since the Roman Empire. Their water, rich in sulfur, radon, and other minerals, has a temperature of about 39°C and is considered healing. Visitors first take mud baths and then immerse themselves in hot thermal pools.

And the "beach" is what happens next. Directly from the wooden piers of the thermal complex, you can step down into the crystal-clear and cool waters of Köyceğiz Lake. This contrast of temperatures — from hot to cold — gives an incredible boost of energy, tones the skin, and leaves unforgettable sensations.

Beach and Infrastructure
Coastline: There is no beach as such here. The shore is rocky, but for the convenience of visitors, wooden piers and platforms with stairs for entering the water have been built.

Infrastructure: All infrastructure belongs to the thermal complex. There are changing rooms, showers, toilets, and a simple cafe. The complex is operated by the Köyceğiz municipality.

Atmosphere: Healing, relaxed, but at the same time lively due to the constant flow of excursion boats from Dalyan and Köyceğiz.

How to get there
By water (main way): Most visitors arrive as part of boat tours from Dalyan or Köyceğiz. This is the most scenic and easy route.

By land: There is also a road from Köyceğiz that goes along a scenic but winding route around the lake.

Pros and Cons
Pros:
A unique experience of combining hot springs and a cool lake.
Health benefits from mineral waters and muds.
Stunningly beautiful views of the lake and mountains.
Part of the classic route of boat excursions in Dalyan.

Cons:
Absence of a traditional beach for sunbathing.
It can be very crowded during peak hours when large tour groups arrive.
Specific smell of hydrogen sulfide from the hot springs.

Who is Sultaniye "beach" suitable for?
This is a place for those looking for something more than just swimming. It is ideal for spa lovers, those who want to improve their health, and everyone who loves unusual natural phenomena. This is a must-visit point in the excursion program of Dalyan and Köyceğiz Lake, which allows you to combine business with pleasure and get completely new sensations from recreation on the water.
