Yuvarlakçay — Ice river and giant swings in a green canyon
Imagine a hot summer day when the asphalt is melting and the air is still. Now imagine a place where you can cool down in ice-cold water in a matter of seconds, have lunch right over a churning stream, and ride a giant swing. This is Yuvarlakçay — one of the most unusual and fun "natural" attractions on the entire Aegean coast.

Main Feature: Ice water and swings over the river
Ice water: The water in the Yuvarlakçay River, which originates from mountain springs, is only +6-8°C all year round. It is impossible to swim here for long, but plunging for a few seconds gives an incredible charge of energy and adrenaline. The screams and shrieks of those jumping into the water for the first time are the signature soundtrack of this place.

Giant swings: The main attraction that made this place famous. Large wooden swings are installed over the river. You swing and, at the highest point, jump directly into the icy water. It is fun, a little scary, and very photogenic.

Restaurants over water
Several restaurants are located along the gorge, offering a unique experience. Their main feature is wooden platforms and gazebos built on stilts directly over the river. You dine in the cool, literally a couple of centimeters from the running water. The main dishes on the menu are grilled freshly caught trout (alabalık) and traditional Turkish village breakfast (köy kahvaltısı).

Atmosphere and Tips
Atmosphere: Very lively, noisy (in a good way), family-oriented. On weekends, it can be very crowded, as this is a favorite holiday destination for locals from Marmaris, Fethiye, and surrounding villages.

Tip: Come on a weekday if you want to avoid the crowds. Do not forget your swimsuit and towel. Be prepared for the fact that the water is indeed VERY cold.

Pros and Cons
Pros:
A unique and fun experience (especially the swings).
An ideal escape from the summer heat.
Beautiful nature and unusual restaurants.
Delicious local food (especially trout).

Cons:
Extremely cold water, not suitable for long swimming.
Can be very crowded and noisy on weekends.
Transport is required to get there.

Who is Yuvarlakçay suitable for?
Yuvarlakçay is an ideal place for a day trip to diversify your beach holiday. It will appeal to families with teenagers, young people, and anyone looking for unusual impressions and not afraid of cold water. If you want to see the real, non-touristy Turkey, have fun, and make cool photos for social networks, then a trip to Yuvarlakçay will be one of the brightest memories of your vacation.
