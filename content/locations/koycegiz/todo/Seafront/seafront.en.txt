# Köyceğiz Seafront (Köyceğiz Kordonu) — Promenade of Cittaslow Serenity

Köyceğiz Seafront is a shady lakeside promenade lined with eucalyptus trees, cozy cafes, boat tour piers, and gorgeous sunset views over mountain ridges.

As [Köyceğiz](/en/koycegiz) is an official member of the international "slow cities" network (Cittaslow), the local waterfront promenade (Kordon) perfectly embodies this philosophy. You won't find loud bars or flashy neon signs here. Instead, it is a serene haven for peaceful strolls along the shores of the tectonic Lake [Köyceğiz](/en/koycegiz).

### Strolling Along the Kordon
The promenade extends for about 2 km along the lake. The pedestrian area is beautifully paved and accompanied by a dedicated bicycle lane:
* **Eastern Section (Urban):** Starts at the main town square near the Atatürk monument. This area hosts traditional fish restaurants and tea gardens (Çay Bahçesi) offering lake views. The main pier is also located here, serving as the departure point for boats sailing to [Dalyan](/en/dalyan).
* **Western Section (Natural):** As you walk towards [Delta Beach](/en/koycegiz/beach/urbanbeach), the promenade becomes greener and quieter, shaded by giant eucalyptus and plane trees. Wooden piers with benches protrude into the lake, inviting visitors to sit or climb down for a freshwater swim.

### Practical Information
* **Location:** Town waterfront of Köyceğiz.
* **Road:** Flat, paved municipal street with separate pedestrian and bicycle paths.
* **Infrastructure:** Cafes, tea gardens, restaurants, benches, playgrounds, bicycle rentals, wooden swimming piers, Atatürk Square.
* **Entrance:** Free.
* **Tip:** Arrive half an hour before sunset — the sun dips slowly behind the mountain peaks on the opposite side of the lake, bathing the entire scenery in a spectacular golden glow.
