# Ekincik Bay (Ekincik Koyu) — Emerald Yacht Harbor and Quiet Beach

Ekincik Bay is a scenic gulf in Köyceğiz featuring a pebble beach, surrounded by pine forests, highly popular among yachtsmen and seekers of quiet nature.

This incredibly beautiful harbor is located approximately 35 km south of the town of [Köyceğiz](/en/koycegiz). Surrounded by steep mountains covered in thick pine forests, the bay is a favorite anchorage for yachts and traditional Turkish gulets sailing the Aegean Sea (as part of the famous "Blue Cruise"). Here, the sea meets untouched wilderness, creating excellent conditions for a tranquil getaway.

### Beach and Sea
The beach is long and composed of dark sand and large pebbles. The water in the bay is exceptionally clear with a deep emerald-blue hue. The water depth increases rather quickly, so parents with small children should remain cautious. During the peak summer heat, the pebbles get very hot, so water shoes are highly recommended for comfortable entry.

### Infrastructure
A well-maintained municipal beach operates on the coast, featuring a cafe-restaurant run by the Köyceğiz Municipality. Here you can rent sunbeds and umbrellas, and use the free showers, toilets, and changing cabins. Nearby is a municipal campground for tents and RVs, along with several private pensions. On the eastern slope of the bay sits the famous My Marina restaurant, renowned for its panoramic views of the harbor and fresh seafood.

### Things to Do
* **Beach Relaxation:** Enjoy the pristine sea in a calm and quiet environment.
* **Boat Tours:** Rent a small boat to explore local sea caves or sail to the mouth of the [Dalyan](/en/dalyan) River and visit the ancient ruins of [Kaunos](/en/koycegiz/ruine/kaunos).
* **Hiking:** Walk along sections of the Lycian Way and forest trails surrounding the bay, which offer spectacular vistas of the gulf.

### Practical Information
* **Location:** Ekincik Bay, 35 km from Köyceğiz.
* **Road:** Scenic but narrow and winding paved asphalt road passing through mountain terrain and Hamitköy village. Careful driving is required.
* **Infrastructure:** Municipal cafe, toilets, showers, changing cabins, sunbeds/umbrellas for rent, campground, pensions, and restaurants.
* **Entrance:** Free. Parking at the municipal lot has a small fee (around 50–100 TL per day).
* **Tip:** If you enjoy fresh seafood and stunning viewpoints, book a table at the My Marina restaurant for sunset, but be prepared for prices that are higher than average.
