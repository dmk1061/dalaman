Delta Beach — Family oasis on the shores of Köyceğiz Lake
In the very heart of the cozy town of Köyceğiz, where the picturesque promenade lined with green eucalyptus trees ends, lies Delta Beach (Delta Plajı). This is not just a place for swimming, but the soul of summer recreation for locals and the main point of attraction for families with children. It is a perfect example of what a comfortable and safe holiday on the shores of a freshwater lake can be.

Main Feature: Safety and family atmosphere
Delta Beach is synonymous with "family vacation." Its main advantages are:

Shallow water: The lake is very shallow here. You have to walk dozens of meters for the water to reach your waist. This creates an absolutely safe environment where even the smallest children can splash near the shore without fear.

Warm fresh water: The absence of waves, salt, and sea creatures makes swimming as comfortable as possible. The lake water warms up beautifully in the summer.

Tranquility: There is no loud music or noisy bars here. The atmosphere is very calm and conducive to relaxation.

Beach and Infrastructure
Coastline: The beach is a small, well-kept strip of sand and very fine gravel. It is located in a picturesque spot, with the endless surface of the lake on one side and a shady eucalyptus grove on the other.

Infrastructure: The beach is run by the municipality, which guarantees cleanliness and very affordable prices. Here is everything you need:

Cafe: A simple municipal cafe offers drinks, ice cream, and light snacks at reasonable prices.

Sun loungers and umbrellas: Can be rented for the whole day for a symbolic fee.

Picnic area: Next to the beach, in the shade of the trees, is a large picnic area with tables and benches.

Camping: Next door is a popular municipal campsite, making the beach convenient for those traveling with a tent or caravan.

What to do
Swimming and games with children: Enjoy safe swimming in warm water.

Picnic: Arrange a family lunch in the shady grove.

Walking: Combine relaxing on the beach with a leisurely walk or bike ride along the beautiful promenade of Köyceğiz.

People-watching: This is an excellent place to watch the slow-paced life of a Turkish provincial town.

Pros and Cons
Pros:
Ideal and safe for children.
Very affordable prices for all services.
Calm and authentic atmosphere.
Warm, fresh, clean water.
Shady picnic area available.

Cons:
It is a lake, not the sea.
The beach is small and can be quite busy, especially on weekends due to locals.
The infrastructure is simple, without luxury.

Who is Delta Beach suitable for?
Delta Beach is the absolute number one choice for families with small children vacationing in Köyceğiz. It will also appeal to anyone looking for a calm, budget-friendly, and safe water holiday away from crowded sea resorts. If your goal is to relax in a beautiful place while the children play safely in the water, Delta Beach will be a real find for you.
