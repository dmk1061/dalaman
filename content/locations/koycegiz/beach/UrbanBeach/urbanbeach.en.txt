# Delta Beach (Delta Plajı) — Municipal Family Beach in Köyceğiz

Delta Beach is a cozy municipal beach on Köyceğiz Lake, offering a safe shallow entrance, a shaded eucalyptus picnic grove, and an affordable family cafe.

This popular beach is located in the southern part of [Köyceğiz](/en/koycegiz) town, right where the scenic eucalyptus [seafront](/en/koycegiz/todo/seafront) ends. Being a municipal facility, Delta Beach is a favorite getaway spot for both local families and tourists traveling with children.

### Safety and Conditions for Children
The main highlight of the beach is its exceptionally gentle, sandy lake bed. Shallow water extends for dozens of meters from the shore, meaning the water is always warm and completely free of waves. This creates an absolutely safe environment for toddlers to splash around. The beach strip consists of a mix of soft sand and fine gravel.

### Infrastructure and Amenities
The beach provides all the necessary amenities for a comfortable day out at very budget-friendly municipal rates:
* **Municipal Cafe:** Serves tea, coffee, cold drinks, ice cream, and light snacks at regulated public prices.
* **Sunbeds and Umbrellas:** Available for rent for the entire day for a very modest fee.
* **Picnic Area:** Under the shade of tall eucalyptus trees next to the beach, there is a dedicated picnic zone with wooden tables and benches.
* **Campground:** A secure municipal campground for caravans and tents is located right next to the beach.

### Practical Information
* **Location:** Southern end of the Köyceğiz seafront, within town limits.
* **Road:** Paved town streets. Easily reachable on foot from the town center or by car.
* **Infrastructure:** Cafe, toilets, showers, changing cabins, sunbed/umbrella rentals, picnic tables, campground.
* **Entrance:** Free. Parking on adjacent streets is free.
* **Tip:** The beach gets quite crowded on weekends with local families. For a quieter atmosphere, plan your visit for weekdays or during the morning hours.
