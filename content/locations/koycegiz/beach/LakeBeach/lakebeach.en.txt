Köyceğiz Lake Beach — Freshwater oasis at the foot of the mountains
Lake Beach (Göl Plajı) in Köyceğiz is a unique alternative to traditional seaside holidays. Located on the shores of the huge tectonic Köyceğiz Lake, this beach offers swimming in calm fresh water while admiring majestic mountain peaks and green shores. This place is ideal for those who want to take a break from salty seawater and enjoy the atmosphere of a quiet lake resort.

Key Features: Fresh water and thermal heritage
Freshwater swimming: The water in Köyceğiz Lake is fresh, with a slight touch of mineral salts due to the thermal springs flowing into it. It is soft and excellently refreshing in the summer heat.

Thermal springs: The lake is fed by the hot springs of Sultaniye, rich in radon and sulfur. The lake water is believed to have healing properties and be beneficial for the skin.

Tranquility without waves: There are never strong surfs or storms here. The lake surface is usually calm as a mirror, making swimming safe even for the smallest children.

Promenade and nature: Along the beach runs a picturesque promenade (Kordon) with sprawling palms, eucalyptus, and rare sweetgum (liquidambar) trees.

Beach and Infrastructure
Coastline: The shore is sandy-pebbly, in some places grassy. The bottom of the lake is sandy and gradual, though in some spots it can be muddy.

Infrastructure: The municipal beach is well-equipped. There are changing rooms, showers, toilets, and sun lounger and umbrella rentals at very affordable prices. Cozy cafes and tea gardens (Çay Bahçesi) operate along the promenade, where it is pleasant to drink a glass of Turkish tea.

What to do
Swimming and relaxation: Relaxing holiday on the shore of the lake.
Water sports: Rent kayaks, paddleboards (SUP), and pedal boats for water excursions.
Watching the sunset: Sunsets over Köyceğiz Lake are considered some of the most beautiful in the region — the sun slowly sinks behind the mountains, coloring the water in all shades of pink and gold.

Pros and Cons
Pros:
Unique experience of swimming in a fresh, mineralized lake.
Safe water without waves, ideal for children.
Low prices for equipment rental and in beachside cafes.
Stunning views of the mountains and a picturesque green promenade.

Cons:
The water in the lake is not as transparently turquoise as in the Aegean Sea (it has a greenish hue).
In summer, due to still water, there may be more midges and mosquitoes, especially in the evening.
For lovers of classic sandy sea beaches, a lake holiday may seem unusual.

Who is Lake Beach suitable for?
The beach on Köyceğiz Lake will appeal to families with children, lovers of calm contemplative recreation, and those seeking variety. It is a wonderful place to spend a quiet day in nature, ride a paddleboard on the mirror-like surface of the lake, and end the evening with dinner on a cozy promenade with a view of the setting sun.
