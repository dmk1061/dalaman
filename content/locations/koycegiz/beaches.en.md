Köyceğiz, located on the shores of a picturesque lake rather than the sea, offers unique beach holiday opportunities. While there are no traditional sea beaches in the town itself, there are excellent alternatives both on the lake and in its immediate vicinity.

### **Beaches on Köyceğiz Lake**

Although Köyceğiz Lake is not a sea, its shores offer several spots for swimming and relaxing by the water. These are small, often wild or well-equipped beaches where you can enjoy the peace and beauty of nature.

*   [🏛️ <u>**Köyceğiz Urban Beach**</u>](/koycegiz/beach/UrbanBeach): A small strip of waterfront in the town itself, where you can swim in the fresh and calm waters of the lake. This spot is popular among locals.
*   [🏊‍♂️ <u>**Akçagerme Beach Club**</u>](/koycegiz/beach/LakeBeach): Located on the road towards Kalkan, this beach club offers a pebble beach, sun loungers, umbrellas, a safe swimming area, and water slides for children.

### **Sea Beaches in the Vicinity**

For those who prefer saltwater and sea waves, the nearest beaches are in the Dalyan area, which can be easily reached by boat or car.

*   **Iztuzu Beach (İztuzu Plajı):** One of the most famous and beautiful beaches in Turkey, located about a 30-40 minute drive from Köyceğiz. This 4.5-kilometer sandy beach is a unique place, as it is washed by the Mediterranean Sea on one side and the fresh waters of the Dalyan River delta on the other. Iztuzu is also an important nesting site for Caretta caretta loggerhead sea turtles, so part of the beach is closed to visitors at night during the nesting season. The beach is well-equipped with cafes, showers, toilets, and sun lounger rentals.

*   [⛵ <u>**Ekincik Bay**</u>](/koycegiz/beach/Ekincik): This picturesque bay with pristine water and beautiful scenery is a popular spot for yachting enthusiasts and those looking for a secluded getaway. There is a small but cozy beach here where you can relax and enjoy nature. You can reach the bay by car or by boat from Köyceğiz or Dalyan.

Thus, a holiday in Köyceğiz allows you to combine the pleasures of both lake and sea coasts, offering diverse options for beach holiday lovers.
