## Marmaris Beach Guide: From the Center to Hidden Bays

Marmaris and its surroundings are famous for a variety of beaches that can satisfy any taste. Here you can find both lively urban beaches with developed infrastructure and secluded bays with crystal clear water surrounded by pine forests.

### Urban Beaches

#### 1. [🏖️ **<u>Marmaris Urban Beach</u>**](/marmaris/beach/UrbanBeach)
Stretching along the entire central promenade, this beach is the most accessible and popular. It is fairly narrow and covered with dark sand and pebbles. The entrance to the water is gradual, which is convenient for families with children. Countless cafes, bars, and restaurants are located along the beach, providing their sun loungers and umbrellas free of charge when ordering drinks or food. This is an ideal place for those who want to combine a beach holiday with city life.

#### 2. [🏖️ **<u>Icmeler Beach</u>**](/marmaris/beach/Icmeler)
Just 8 km from the center of Marmaris is the resort village of İçmeler with the beach of the same name. It is considered one of the best in the region. The beach here is wider, and the sand is lighter and finer than in the center of Marmaris. The water is very clean and transparent, of a beautiful turquoise color. The infrastructure is top-notch: there are water sports equipment rentals, showers, changing cabins, and many cozy establishments.

### Beaches in the Surroundings

#### 3. [🏖️ **<u>Turunç Beach</u>**](/marmaris/beach/Turunc)
Located in a picturesque bay 20 km from Marmaris, Turunç Beach has been awarded the "Blue Flag" for its cleanliness. This is a wonderful place for a quiet holiday. The beach is covered with fine pebbles mixed with sand, and the water here is so clear that the bottom is visible several meters deep. You can get here by dolmuş (minibus) or by water taxi from Marmaris.

#### 4. [🏝️ **<u>Cleopatra Beach (Sedir Island)</u>**](/marmaris/beach/CleopatraSedir)
A true gem of the region. Legend has it that the unique white sand for this beach was brought from Egypt specifically for Queen Cleopatra. The sand has a special structure and is strictly protected by the state — it is forbidden to remove it from the beach. The water here is of an unreal turquoise color. A trip to Sedir Island is a full-day excursion that includes a boat trip and a chance to experience history.

#### 5. [🏊‍♂️ **<u>Amos Bay</u>**](/marmaris/beach/AmosBay)
A small and cozy bay hidden from crowds of tourists. Here you will find a pebble beach with crystal clear water, ideal for snorkeling. On a hill next to the bay, the ruins of the ancient city of Amos are preserved, offering a spectacular view of the surroundings.

#### 6. [🏖️ **<u>Çiftlik</u>**](/marmaris/beach/Ciftlik)
The beach in Çiftlik Bay attracts lovers of privacy and beautiful nature. It is covered with coarse sand and lies far from the noise of resort centers. This spot is popular among yachtsmen who often stop here.

### Additional Beaches of the Region

#### 7. [🪁 **<u>Akyaka Beach</u>**](/marmaris/beach/Akyaka)
A cozy beach in a picturesque bay, known for its calm waters and excellent conditions for kitesurfing.

#### 8. [🏞️ **<u>Çiftebük Beach</u>**](/marmaris/beach/Ciftebuk)
A quiet bay for lovers of a secluded holiday far from tourist crowds.

---

Whichever beach you choose, Marmaris will give you an unforgettable holiday on the coast where the Aegean and Mediterranean seas meet.
