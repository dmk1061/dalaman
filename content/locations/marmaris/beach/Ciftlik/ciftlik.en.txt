Çiftlik Beach — Sandy harbor for the perfect escape
If you are looking for a beautiful sandy beach, clean open sea, and a calm rustic atmosphere, Çiftlik Beach (Çiftlik Plajı) is an excellent choice. Located about 30 kilometers south of Marmaris, this cozy bay is famous for its large coarse sand, which is a rarity for this mostly pebbly region, and its stunning natural beauty.

Main Feature: Large sand and open sea
Coarse Sand: The beach in Çiftlik is covered with beautiful large sand of a dark golden hue. It is pleasant to walk on, and it does not stick to the skin as much as fine sand. This makes the beach very comfortable for relaxation.

Open Sea: Unlike the closed bay of Marmaris, Çiftlik Bay faces the open sea. Because of this, the water here is exceptionally clean and has a deep blue-turquoise color. Even on hot summer days, the water here remains refreshingly cool.

Yacht Stop: The bay is very popular among yachtsmen. Beautiful yachts and traditional Turkish gulets are constantly anchored here, which adds additional charm and a touch of romance to the landscape.

Beach and Infrastructure
Coastline: The beach is long and relatively wide. Entering the water is gradual, but depth starts rather quickly. The bottom near the shore is mostly sandy.

Infrastructure: The beach is well-equipped. Most of the coastline is divided between coastal restaurants and hotels, which offer sun loungers and umbrellas (often free of charge if you order food or drinks). There are changing cabins, showers, and toilets. Along the beach, there are several excellent fish restaurants where you can dine right by the water.

What to do
Swimming and sunbathing: Enjoy the sandy beach and clean sea.

Boat trips: Rent a boat to visit neighboring secluded bays or the nearby island.

Gastronomic relaxation: Try fresh seafood at one of the beachside restaurants.

Diving: The clean waters of the bay are popular for diving and snorkeling.

Pros and Cons
Pros:
Excellent sandy beach, rare for the Marmaris region.
Very clean, clear, open sea.
Calm, relaxed atmosphere of a seaside village.
Good selection of fish restaurants right on the beach.

Cons:
The water can be cooler than in the closed bay of Marmaris.
In the height of the season, it can be crowded due to day tourists and boats.
The road from Marmaris is scenic but winding (takes about 45 minutes by car).

Who is Çiftlik Beach suitable for?
Çiftlik Beach is ideal for families, romantic couples, and independent travelers looking for a comfortable beach holiday with beautiful nature and good food, but away from the noise of a large resort. It will appeal to those who value a clean sea, a quality sandy beach, and slow lunches with a view of yachts on the horizon.
