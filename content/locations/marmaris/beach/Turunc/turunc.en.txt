Turunc — A Cozy Bay with a Blue Flag
Located 20 km from Marmaris, at the end of a picturesque mountain serpentine, Turunc is a true oasis of tranquility. Its name translates as "bitter orange," recalling the citrus groves that once grew here. Today, it is a small but very well-maintained resort that prides itself on its cleanliness, hospitality, and stunning nature.

Main Feature: Blue Flag and Village Charm
The key difference of Turunc is its beach, which has been awarded the prestigious "Blue Flag." This certificate guarantees high standards of water cleanliness, safety, and environmental management. The water here is indeed crystal clear and calm.

The second feature is the atmosphere. Turunc has preserved the charm of a small fishing village. There are no high-rise hotels or noisy nightclubs here. Instead, you'll find low-rise buildings, family-run hotels, cozy restaurants, and a friendly, almost homely environment.

Beach and Scenery
Coastline: The crescent-shaped beach consists of a mix of sand and pebbles. It is well-equipped and well-maintained. The sea in the bay is very calm, with no waves, which is ideal for swimming.

Scenery: Like Icmeler, Turunc is surrounded by steep, pine-covered mountains. The scenery here is perhaps even more dramatic, as the bay is more enclosed and cozy.

Infrastructure and What to Do
Strolls: In the center of the village, there is one main street leading to the sea, lined with shops, souvenir stalls, and a small market on Mondays.

Restaurants: A wide selection of restaurants and bars along the promenade offering both Turkish and European cuisine.

Boat Trips: Boat taxis and excursions depart from Turunc's small harbor to the neighboring, even more secluded bays of Amos and Kumlubuk.

Active Recreation: The area is excellent for hiking along mountain trails. There are also diving centers here.

Pros and Cons
Pros:

Blue Flag beach, guaranteeing water cleanliness.
Very calm, safe, and friendly atmosphere.
Magnificent natural scenery.
Ideal for a quiet family holiday.

Cons:

Limited night life.
The road from Marmaris is a rather steep and winding serpentine.
The scale of the resort is small, which may seem boring to lovers of active entertainment.

Who is Turunc for?
Turunc is an ideal spot for those looking for maximum relaxation and communion with nature, without sacrificing comfort. It is perfect for families with small children (due to the calm sea and safe atmosphere) and older couples. This is the choice of those who find Icmeler too noisy and Marmaris too bustling. If your goal is a week of silence, clean sea, and beautiful views, Turunc will be a real discovery for you.
