Çiftebük Beach — A hidden paradise for wild nature lovers
If you are looking for absolute privacy, clean sea, and want to feel like a Robinson Crusoe away from civilization, Çiftebük Beach (Çiftebük Plajı) is exactly what you need. Located on the Datça Peninsula, a few dozen kilometers from Marmaris, this wild beach is one of the last corners of pristine nature, untouched by mass tourism.

Main Feature: Wild beauty and isolation
Robinson's Refuge: There are no hotels, beach clubs, or shops here. Çiftebük is a completely wild beach. You will not find umbrellas and loungers here, only the clean sea, pebbles, and pine forests coming right to the water.

Two Bays: The name "Çiftebük" translates from Turkish as "Double Bay." Indeed, the beach consists of two small adjacent bays separated by a scenic rocky promontory. This creates additional opportunities to find a completely isolated spot.

Cleanest Water: Due to the lack of infrastructure and the distance from large settlements, the water here is amazingly clear and has a beautiful turquoise-blue hue. It is an ideal place for swimming and snorkeling.

Beach and Infrastructure
Coastline: The beach is covered with pebbles of various sizes, sometimes mixed with coarse sand. Entering the water can be steep, and the depth increases quickly. Special swimming shoes are recommended.

Infrastructure: Completely absent. There are no showers, toilets, changing rooms, or cafes. You must bring everything with you: water, food, umbrellas, towels, and swimming shoes.

How to get there
The road to Çiftebük is an adventure in itself. You can get here:

By car: You need to drive along the main road towards Datça, and then turn onto a dirt forest road leading to the bay. The road can be steep and uneven, so a car with high ground clearance is recommended.

By water: You can rent a boat in Marmaris or neighboring bays to get to Çiftebük by sea.

Pros and Cons
Pros:
Absolute privacy and tranquility, far from tourist crowds.
Stunningly beautiful nature, clean air, and crystal-clear sea.
Opportunity to feel like a pioneer.
Ideal place for camping and overnight stays in a tent.

Cons:
Complete lack of infrastructure and amenities.
Difficult access (dirt and steep road).
The depth increases quickly, which is not safe for small children.
You must carry all trash back with you to preserve this place.

Who is Çiftebük Beach suitable for?
Çiftebük is suitable for independent travelers, active tourists, camping lovers, and everyone who wants to escape the noise of civilization. It will appeal to those who value pristine nature, silence, and the cleanliness of the sea more than comfort and service. If you are ready for a small trip along a forest road for the sake of a private bay, Çiftebük will be a real reward for you.
