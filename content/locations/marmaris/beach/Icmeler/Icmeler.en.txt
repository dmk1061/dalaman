Icmeler Beach — Turquoise harmony with a taste of comfort
If the central beach of Marmaris seems too busy and narrow, but you do not want to give up comfort, developed infrastructure, and close proximity to the city, İçmeler Beach (İçmeler Plajı) is the perfect solution. Located just 8 kilometers west of Marmaris in a scenic bay of the same name, this beach is rightfully considered one of the best and cleanest in the region.

Main Feature: Golden middle between nature and comfort
Clean Water: Unlike the central bay of Marmaris, the water in İçmeler is cleaner, more transparent, and has a beautiful turquoise hue. This is due to a more active water circulation in this part of the bay.

Stunning Views: The bay is surrounded by high mountains covered with thick pine forests. From the shore, a magnificent view opens up of the islands and yachts floating on the water, creating a feeling of cozy seclusion despite the popularity of the resort.

Wide sandy beach: The beach here is wider than in the center of Marmaris, and the sand is lighter and finer, although there is still a small amount of pebbles near the shore.

Infrastructure and Entertainment
Excellent Promenade: A beautiful pedestrian promenade lined with palms and flowers runs along the beach, connecting İçmeler with Marmaris. It is a popular place for walks and bicycle rides.

Developed Infrastructure: There are changing rooms, showers, toilets, and numerous cafes and restaurants. Most of the beach is equipped with sun loungers and umbrellas.

Water sports: İçmeler is a center for active recreation. Here you can rent jet skis, go parasailing, ride a "banana" boat, or rent a pedal boat.

What to do
Relaxing beach holiday: Ideal for spending the whole day by the water.

Walk to Marmaris: Take a walk or ride a bicycle along the promenade.

Boat trip: Rent a taxi boat to visit neighboring bays or Turunç.

Pros and Cons
Pros:
Clean and transparent water of turquoise color.
Beautiful views of the mountains and islands.
Developed infrastructure and a wide selection of entertainment.
Convenient connection with Marmaris (regular dolmuşes and taxi boats).

Cons:
Can be very crowded during peak hours in the summer.
Prices in cafes and for sun lounger rentals are higher than on the central beach of Marmaris.
The entry into the water can be cool due to underwater freshwater springs.

Who is İçmeler Beach suitable for?
İçmeler Beach is suitable for families with children, romantic couples, and everyone who appreciates a high level of comfort and cleanliness of the sea, but wants to stay close to urban infrastructure. This is an ideal place for those who seek the "golden middle" between a wild beach and a city resort.
