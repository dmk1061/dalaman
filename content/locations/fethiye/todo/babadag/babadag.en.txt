Babadağ — The Sky Gate of Ölüdeniz
Mount Babadağ, towering nearly 2000 meters directly over the turquoise waters of Ölüdeniz, is not just a natural landmark, but a real magnet for adventure seekers and beauty lovers from all over the world. Its unique location and weather conditions have made it a legend in the world of paragliding and a place offering the most breathtaking views of the Lycian coast.

Main Feature: Paragliding and Cable Car
World capital of paragliding: Babadağ is considered one of the best and safest places in the world for paragliding. Thanks to stable thermal winds and several launch sites at different altitudes (1700, 1800, and 1969 meters), flights here are possible almost all year round. The bird's-eye view of the Blue Lagoon, Belcekız Beach, and surrounding islands is what thousands of tourists come here for. A tandem flight with an experienced instructor is the main attraction of the region.

Cable car (Babadağ Teleferik): Recently, the climb to the top has become accessible to absolutely everyone thanks to a modern cable car. The ride in a comfortable cabin to an altitude of 1700 meters takes about 20 minutes and is a spectacular excursion in itself with constantly changing panoramic views.

What to Do at the Top?
Even if you do not plan to fly, a trip to the top of Babadağ is a must.

Observation decks: Magnificent observation terraces are equipped at different levels (1200, 1700, 1800, and 1900 meters), offering 360-degree panoramas.

Restaurants and cafes: There are several restaurants and cafes at the top where you can have lunch or a cup of coffee while enjoying the best view in the region. Dining at sunset at an altitude of 1700 meters is a truly unforgettable experience.

Watching paragliders: You can sit a meter away from the launch pad and watch the brave ones run and soar into the sky one by one. This is very spectacular and inspiring.

Pros and Cons
Pros:

Absolutely breathtaking, the best panoramic views of Ölüdeniz and the entire coast.

Opportunity to make a paragliding flight in one of the best places in the world.

A modern and convenient cable car making the summit accessible to everyone.

Excellent infrastructure at the top (restaurants, observation decks).

Cons:

The cost of the cable car ticket is quite high.

It can be significantly colder and windier at the top than down below, even in summer (you need to take warm clothes).

The weather can change quickly, and the summit may be hidden in the clouds.

Who will be interested in Babadağ?
Babadağ is a place for everyone. For thrill-seekers dreaming of flight. For photographers hunting for the best shots. For romantics wishing to meet an unforgettable sunset. For families who want to show their children the world from a bird's-eye view. A trip to the top of Babadağ is the highlight of any holiday in Fethiye or Ölüdeniz, allowing you to fully appreciate the grandiosity and beauty of this unique corner of nature.
