Kıdrak Beach — Pine Paradise in the Shade of Cliffs
Kıdrak Beach (often called "Paradise Bay") is a quiet, picturesque beach located just 2-3 km east of the main resort of Ölüdeniz. Surrounded by pine forests and high cliffs, it offers a peaceful and relaxing alternative to the noisy and crowded beaches of the neighborhood. The territory has the status of a nature park, so entrance here is paid.

Main Feature: Quiet Nature and Pine Scent
Quiet and solitude: Kıdrak is the exact opposite of the neighboring Belcekız Beach. There are no crowds of tourists, loud music, or landing paragliders here. This is a place where you can listen to the sound of the waves and the wind in the pine branches.

Pine park: The beach is adjacent to a beautiful pine grove, which provides natural shade. There are specially equipped picnic areas with tables, benches, and barbecue spots. The combination of the smell of pine needles and the sea breeze creates a unique healing microclimate.

Beach and Sea
Coast: The beach is quite long, covered with a mixture of sand and small light pebbles. Because of this, the water here has an incredible, very clean, bright turquoise color.

Sea: The sea is open here, so there are often waves. The depth increases rather quickly, literally in 2-3 steps from the shore. This should be taken into account when holidaying with children.

Infrastructure and Services
Infrastructure: Despite the status of a nature park, the beach is well-equipped. It has everything you need: a cafe-restaurant, sun lounger and umbrella rentals, showers, toilets, changing cabins, and parking.

Accommodation: Near the beach, there is a luxury hotel complex, but the beach itself remains accessible to all visitors.

Pros and Cons
Pros:

Peaceful, quiet, and relaxed atmosphere.

Beautiful scenery: pine trees, cliffs, turquoise sea.

Excellent picnic facilities in the shade of pine trees.

Clean beach and water.

Cons:

Paid entrance (though the fee is low).

Quick increase in depth and waves (not very convenient for small children).

Lacks active entertainment and water sports.

Who is Kıdrak suitable for?
Kıdrak Beach is an ideal place for those who want to escape from the crowds and noise of Ölüdeniz, but do not want to sacrifice comfort. It is perfect for couples, nature lovers, those who like to read a book in the shade of trees, and families who want to have a picnic in nature by the sea. If you value peace, clean water, and the smell of pine trees, Kıdrak will be a real find for you.
