# Kidrak Beach (Kıdrak Koyu) — A Peaceful Pine Haven Neighboring Ölüdeniz
A secluded sand and pebble beach in a nature reserve, bordered by dense pine forests and offering a tranquil escape away from the resort crowds.

Kidrak Beach is a hidden gem located just a few kilometers away from the busy [Belcekiz Beach](/en/fethiye/beach/belcekiz) in Ölüdeniz. Set within the Kidrak Natural Park (Kıdrak Tabiat Parkı), it provides a completely different experience — peaceful, slow-paced, and private. There is no loud music or mass tourist groups here; instead, you are surrounded by the sound of waves and singing cicadas.

The defining feature of Kidrak is the majestic pine trees growing almost at the water's edge. They offer generous natural shade to hide from the sun and fill the air with a fresh pine aroma. The area behind the beach is set up as a recreational picnic zone (mesire alanı) with tables and BBQ spots, making it a favorite for family gatherings.

The shore consists of a mix of sand and clean pebbles. The sea is exceptionally clear and features a beautiful azure color. Unlike the sheltered [Blue Lagoon](/en/fethiye/beach/bluelagoon), this is an open sea coast, meaning the water gets deep quickly and waves can occur. The park has a small cafe, toilets, showers, changing cabins, and sunbed rentals.

### Practical Information
- Location: 3 km southeast of Ölüdeniz, 17 km from [Fethiye](/en/fethiye)
- Road: Asphalt (easily reachable by car, taxi, or the dolmuş heading towards Faralya/Kabak)
- Infrastructure: Cafe, toilets, showers, changing cabins, sunbed and umbrella rentals, picnic tables, and BBQ zones
- Entrance: Paid (pedestrian entry is approx. 85 TL, car entry including parking is approx. 255 TL; Museum Card / Müze Kart is not accepted)
- Tip: Bring water shoes for a more comfortable entry into the sea over the pebbles. If you plan to have a picnic under the pines, it is best to buy your groceries and charcoal beforehand in Fethiye.
