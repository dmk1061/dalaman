Blue Lagoon — The Natural Pool of Ölüdeniz
The Blue Lagoon of Ölüdeniz is a natural treasure protected by the state. It is an almost completely enclosed bay separated from the open sea by a magnificent sandy spit. It is this spit that creates the unique conditions so valued by holidaymakers. Entrance to the territory of the lagoon and the spit is paid, as it is a national park.

Main Feature: Tranquility and Safety
The main difference between the Blue Lagoon and the main Belcekız Beach is its water.

Natural pool: Thanks to the protection of the sandy spit, the water in the lagoon is almost always absolutely calm, without the slightest waves. This creates the feeling of swimming in a huge natural pool filled with sea water.

Shallow water and warm water: The entry into the water is very gentle, and the depth increases slowly. The water here warms up much better than in the open sea. All this makes the lagoon the safest and most comfortable place for swimming for small children.

Two Shores of One Spit
Kumburnu Beach, which forms the lagoon, is unique in that it has two different shores:

Inner shore (lagoon side): Here is the very "paddling pool" — shallow, warm, and calm water, sand. Ideal for children.

Outer shore (open sea side): If you cross the spit (it takes only a minute), you will find yourself on a shore washed by the waves of the open sea, just like on Belcekız Beach. This allows you to get two different swimming experiences in one visit.

Infrastructure and Atmosphere
Infrastructure: On the territory of the park there is everything you need: several cafes, sun lounger and umbrella rentals (the cost is usually higher than on the public beach), showers, toilets, changing cabins. You can also rent a pedal boat or canoe for a trip around the lagoon.

Atmosphere: Very relaxed, peaceful, and family-oriented. There is no noise from bars or landing paragliders here. This is a place for a quiet holiday and enjoying natural beauty.

Pros and Cons
Pros:

Ideal conditions for families with small children (calm, shallow, warm).

Incredibly beautiful landscapes.

Opportunity to swim both in the calm lagoon and in the open sea.

Sandy areas, unlike the pebbly main beach.

Cons:

Paid entrance.

It can be very crowded, especially in the shallow water.

Prices for services inside the park are higher than outside.

The water in the furthest and most stagnant part of the lagoon can sometimes seem less transparent due to the lack of circulation.

Who is the Blue Lagoon suitable for?
The Blue Lagoon is the number one choice for everyone who comes to Ölüdeniz with small children. It is also an ideal place for those who do not like waves and prefer to swim in calm, warm water. If you want to spend a day in one of the most beautiful and safest places for swimming in Turkey and are willing to pay for it, then the Blue Lagoon will give you exactly the serene holiday you are looking for.
