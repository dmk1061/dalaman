Kabak Bay — The Last Hippie Stronghold on the Lycian Way
Kabak Bay is a legendary place, a mecca for backpackers, yogis, nature lovers, and anyone looking for a "different" Turkey, far from five-star hotels and package tours. Located in a deep gorge descending to the sea, this valley has preserved its wild beauty and unique bohemian atmosphere largely due to its remoteness.

Main Feature: Atmosphere of Freedom and Unity with Nature
Kabak is not about luxury, but about simplicity and freedom. There are no large hotels here; instead, there are numerous campsites, yoga retreats, and simple wooden bungalows scattered along the slopes of the gorge. Life here flows at its own leisurely pace. During the day — relaxing on the beach, hiking to waterfalls, practicing yoga. In the evening — gatherings around the campfire under a starry sky to the sounds of a guitar. This is a place where people come to "disconnect" from civilization.

Beach and Nature
Coast: The beach in the bay is quite wide (about 200 meters), covered with a mixture of sand and pebbles. The sea here is open, very clean, of a beautiful turquoise color, but there are often waves, and the depth increases quickly.

Nature: The valley itself is a separate attraction. A stream flows through it, forming small waterfalls and pools where you can swim. The bay is part of the famous hiking route "Lycian Way".

How to Get There
Getting to Kabak is part of the adventure.

By minibus (dolmuş): From Fethiye or Ölüdeniz you need to get to the village of Faralya. The minibus stops at the top of the gorge.

On foot or by "taxi-jeep": From the final stop to the beach itself there are two paths: either go down on foot along a steep path (about 20-30 minutes), or use the services of local drivers in off-road vehicles who shuttle up and down the bumpy road.

Pros and Cons
Pros:

Unique bohemian and relaxed atmosphere.

Stunning wild nature (gorge, waterfalls).

The feeling of a "secret" place, far from mass tourism.

Ideal for lovers of yoga, hiking, and camping.

Cons:

Difficulty of access.

The infrastructure is very simple, not suitable for comfort lovers.

The sea is often wavy and quickly becomes deep, which is not very convenient for families with small children.

Who is Kabak suitable for?
Kabak is a place for a certain type of traveler. It is for those who put nature, freedom, and communication above comfort. It is the perfect choice for backpackers, youth, active recreation lovers, and anyone who wants to see Turkey from an off-the-beaten-path, "hippie" side. If you are looking for five-star service, you definitely do not need to come here. But if you want to wake up in a bungalow to the singing of birds, hike to a waterfall in the afternoon, and sit by a campfire in the evening with people from all over the world, then Kabak can become a real paradise for you.
