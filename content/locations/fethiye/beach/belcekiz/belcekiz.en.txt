Belcekız Beach — The Energetic Heart of Ölüdeniz
Belcekız Beach is the stage where the entire daytime life of Ölüdeniz unfolds. It is a long and wide arc facing the open sea, which serves as a place for relaxation, the main landing pad for paragliders, and a lively promenade. On one side, it borders the cliffs, and on the other, it smoothly transitions into the sandy spit of the famous Blue Lagoon.

Main Feature: Paragliders and Promenade
Paragliding landing strip: The main thing that distinguishes Belcekız from any other beach in the world is the constant "paragliding rain". Dozens of paragliders who started from Mount Babadağ make a soft landing one after another right onto the promenade, in a specially designated zone. Watching this colorful and exciting spectacle, sitting in a cafe or right on the beach, is a unique Ölüdeniz experience.

Lively promenade: A well-kept promenade lined with restaurants, bars, cafes, and souvenir shops runs along the entire beach. In the evening, this is the main place for walks, where you can dine with a view of the sunset.

Beach and Atmosphere
Coast: The beach is covered with light, smooth pebbles. The sea here is open, so there are often small but noticeable waves, and the depth increases quite quickly. The color of the water is a rich, dark turquoise.

Atmosphere: Very lively, energetic, international, and festive. Something is always happening here: paragliders landing, excursion boats departing, music playing in bars. This is a place for those who love to be in the center of events.

Infrastructure: This is a public beach awarded the "Blue Flag" for cleanliness and safety. It has everything: sun lounger and umbrella rentals, showers, toilets, as well as a huge selection of establishments for every taste on the adjacent promenade.

Pros and Cons
Pros:

Incredibly spectacular thanks to the constantly landing paragliders.

Very developed infrastructure with a huge selection of cafes and restaurants.

Beautiful views of the open sea and mountains.

Free admission.

Cons:

Pebble beach, which is not comfortable for everyone.

Frequent waves and quick depth increase, making it less suitable for young children compared to the Blue Lagoon.

It can be very crowded and noisy.

Who is Belcekız suitable for?
Belcekız is a beach for those who love energy and movement. It is ideal for young people, for those who want to be in the center of resort life, and, of course, for everyone who came to Ölüdeniz to paraglide or just watch this exciting spectacle. If you are looking for a calm sea for children, you had better walk a little further into the paid zone of the Blue Lagoon. But if you want to feel the real, vibrant pulse of Ölüdeniz, then Belcekız is exactly the place to listen to it.
