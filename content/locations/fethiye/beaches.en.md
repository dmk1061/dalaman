The Fethiye coast is world-famous for its beaches — from secluded coves to wide sandy spits. Here, everyone will find a place for the perfect holiday by the sea.

### [💙 <u>**Ölüdeniz**</u>](/fethiye/beach/bluelagoon)

Ölüdeniz, or the Blue Lagoon, is the hallmark of not only Fethiye but all of Turkey. This place is in the top 5 best beaches in the world thanks to its unique beauty.

*   **Blue Lagoon:** This is a national reserve with a paid entrance zone. The water here is always calm, like in a swimming pool, even during a storm. An ideal place for families with children.
*   [🏖️ <u>**Belcekız Beach**</u>](/fethiye/beach/belcekiz): A long and wide public beach with a developed infrastructure. Numerous cafes, bars, and restaurants are located here. The beach is the main landing area for paragliders descending from Mount Babadağ.

### Çalış Beach

Located 4 km west of Fethiye city center, this 4-kilometer sand and pebble beach is famous for its incredible sunsets.

*   **Features:** A promenade lined with hotels, restaurants, and bars runs along the beach. Çalış is one of the nesting sites of Caretta Caretta giant turtles. Light winds also blow here, making it a popular spot for windsurfing and kitesurfing.

### [🦋 <u>**Butterfly Valley (Kelebekler Vadisi)**</u>](/fethiye/beach/kelebekler)

A secluded beach located at the foot of Mount Babadağ. You can get here mainly by sea by boat or water taxi from Ölüdeniz.

*   **Features:** The valley got its name due to the large number of butterfly species that can be seen here from June to September. This is a beautiful place for nature lovers, camping, and hiking.

### [🌲 <u>**Kıdrak Beach**</u>](/fethiye/beach/kidrak)

Just 2 km from Ölüdeniz is this quiet and picturesque beach surrounded by pine trees. It is often called "Paradise Bay".

*   **Features:** The beach has an entrance fee, but offers a peaceful atmosphere, crystal-clear water, and all the necessary infrastructure for a comfortable stay, including picnic areas.

### [🧘‍♀️ <u>**Kabak Bay (Kabak Koyu)**</u>](/fethiye/beach/kabakkoyu)

A secluded bay located 25 km from Fethiye. This place is popular among lovers of yoga, trekking, and relaxation in harmony with nature.

*   **Features:** You can reach Kabak via a mountain road. Campgrounds and bungalows predominate here. The beach is pebbly, and the atmosphere is very calm and relaxed.
