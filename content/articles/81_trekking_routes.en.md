# Trekking on the Turquoise Coast: Top Scenic Routes & Trails

While the world-famous **Lycian Way** is the main magnet for multi-day trekkers, the [Dalaman](/en/dalaman) Riviera is interlaced with dozens of other breathtaking trails. From breezy 2-hour coastal walks to technical canyoning routes, here are the top hiking itineraries across the region.

---

## 🧭 Major Long-Distance Networks

### 1. The Carian Trail (Carian Way / Karia Yolu)
If the Lycian Way explores ancient Lycia, the Carian Trail winds through the neighboring kingdoms of ancient Caria. It is a newer, quieter, and noticeably more "wild" alternative.
- **Total Distance:** Approximately 800 km (500 miles).
- **Geography:** Spans the [Datça](/en/dacha) and Bozburun peninsulas, as well as the mountainous interior around Muğla and [Marmaris](/en/marmaris).
- **Highlights:** Passes through ancient olive groves, almond orchards, and secluded pine hills before descending to completely untouched pebble coves. Ideal for hikers seeking solitude away from tourist crowds.

## 🥾 Best Day Hikes & Scenic Trails

### 2. Kayaköy to Ölüdeniz Coastal Overlook
One of the most rewarding half-day hikes on the entire Turquoise Coast.
- **Start point:** The upper ruins of the Ghost Town of Kayaköy.
- **Finish point:** Belcekiz Beach in Ölüdeniz (`Blue Lagoon`).
- **Description:** The trail begins from the abandoned stone church at the top of Kayaköy ridge, winds through aromatic pine forests, and emerges onto a cliffside vantage point offering iconic aerial views of the Blue Lagoon before zigzagging down to the beach.
- **Duration:** 2 to 3 hours (moderate difficulty).

### 3. Kayaköy to Cold Water Bay (`Soğuk Su Koyu`)
A fantastic alternative route starting from the exact same trailhead.
- **Start point:** Ghost Town of Kayaköy.
- **Finish point:** Secluded Cold Water Bay (`Soğuk Su`).
- **Description:** A downhill forest trail leading straight to a crystal-clear bay where refreshing freshwater mountain springs bubble right into the Mediterranean Sea. Perfect for combining a morning hike with a swim.
- **Duration:** 1.5 to 2 hours.

### 4. Saklıkent Gorge River Canyoning
Not a traditional dry-land trek, but an unforgettable adventure inside one of Europe's deepest canyons.
- **Location:** Saklıkent National Park (between [Fethiye](/en/fethiye) and [Kaş](/en/kas)).
- **Description:** Hikers wade directly through icy mountain rapids between 300-meter-high vertical limestone walls. Certain sections require scrambling over boulders and wading waist-deep in cold glacial runoff (+8°C / 46°F).
- **Important:** Sturdy jelly shoes or rubber canyoning boots are mandatory (available for low rental fees at the park entrance).

### 5. Butterfly Valley Cliff Descent (`Faralya`)
> [!WARNING]
> **⚠️ CRITICAL SAFETY WARNING:** This descent is strictly for experienced mountaineers with proper grip footwear and climbing experience!
- **Start point:** Faralya village cliff edge overlooking Butterfly Valley.
- **Finish point:** Butterfly Valley sandy beach.
- **Description:** This is not a standard walking path, but a steep vertical scramble down 350 meters of limestone cliffs utilizing fixed ropes. **The route is objectively dangerous.** For the vast majority of travelers, visiting the valley by boat from Ölüdeniz is infinitely safer.

### 6. Kaş to Liman Ağzı Coastal Path
A delightful and relaxing coastal walk right outside bohemian [Kaş](/en/kas).
- **Start point:** Kaş Marina or Büyük Çakıl beach.
- **Finish point:** Liman Ağzı swimming bays.
- **Description:** A well-marked path tracing limestone sea cliffs, passing ancient Lycian sarcophagi and cave tombs. At the trail's end, you'll arrive at pristine beach clubs where you can swim, enjoy lunch, and take a 10-minute water taxi back to Kaş center.
- **Duration:** 1 to 1.5 hours.
